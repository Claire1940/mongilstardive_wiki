## Troubleshooting Performance Issues

Even if you meet the **mongil star dive minimum requirements android**, you may encounter stuttering. This is often due to background processes or thermal management settings on your phone. To optimize your experience, follow these steps:

1.  **Enable Game Mode:** Most modern Android phones (Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus) have a built-in Game Turbo or Game Booster. Ensure this is active to prioritize CPU/GPU resources.
2.  **Adjust Refresh Rate:** If your screen supports 120Hz, try locking it to 60Hz. This reduces the strain on the GPU and prevents the device from getting too hot.
3.  **Disable Battery Saver:** Android's "Battery Saver" mode severely throttles the CPU. Ensure your phone is in "Performance Mode" or at least "Balanced Mode" while playing.
4.  **Clear Cache:** If the game becomes sluggish after an update, go to Settings > Apps > Mongil Star Dive and clear the cache.

For more information on official updates and community events, check the [official Netmarble website](https://www.netmarble.com) for the latest patch notes and compatibility lists.
