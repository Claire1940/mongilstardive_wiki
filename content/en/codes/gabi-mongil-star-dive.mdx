## Team Synergy and Composition

While **gabi mongil star dive** can handle most solo content with ease, she truly shines when paired with characters who can provide sustained healing or long-range support. Since Gabi spends most of her time in the middle of enemy packs, she is prone to taking chip damage.

### Recommended Teammates

*   **Healers/Buffers:** Characters who can provide damage-over-time healing allow Gabi to maintain her aggressive "smash" playstyle without needing to retreat.
*   **Ranged DPS:** Having a sniper or mage in the party helps pick off flying enemies or those that Gabi cannot easily reach with her club.
*   **Crowd Controllers:** While Gabi has some CC, pairing her with a character that can freeze or stun enemies makes her "Star Dive" ultimate much more effective.

> **Warning:** Avoid pairing Gabi with other "heavy" melee units that lack mobility, as your team may struggle against highly mobile bosses that kite your party.
