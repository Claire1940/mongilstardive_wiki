## Core Stat Priorities for Esther

Esther operates differently than typical burst mages. Her damage profile is heavily weighted toward sustained Basic Attacks and leveraging the "Suppression" state of bosses. When a boss is knocked down, Esther’s class passives trigger, significantly increasing her output. Therefore, Suppression Damage Boost is not just a luxury—it is a mandatory component of her kit.

To build the most effective Esther, you should prioritize your stats in the following order:

1.  **Attack & Crit Rate:** You cannot trigger her set bonuses without consistent critical hits.
2.  **Crit Damage:** Essential for scaling her rapid-fire basic attacks.
3.  **Suppression Damage Boost:** This is her "X-factor" for boss encounters.
4.  **Wind Damage Boost:** Increases all elemental output.
5.  **Elemental Weakness Damage:** Helpful for specific elemental matchups.

> **💡 Tip:** Aim for at least a 60% Crit Rate before focusing heavily on Crit Damage. Esther's best equipment sets rely on "landing a critical hit" to maintain their buffs.

## Skill Leveling and Priority

In Mongil Star Dive, skill points are often limited during the early to mid-game. For an **esther best build**, you must prioritize her Basic Attack above all else. Unlike characters who rely on long-cooldown ultimates, Esther’s primary DPS comes from her "Dust Cloud" mechanics and rapid strikes.

*   **Basic Attack (Priority: Max/Level 12):** This is her bread and butter. The majority of her total damage output comes from her basic combo, especially when enhanced by her signature weapon.
*   **Special Skill (Priority: High/Level 9+):** Using her Special Skill generates Dust Clouds and triggers significant Wind Damage buffs from her gear.
*   **Ultimate Skill (Priority: Medium):** While powerful, it should be used strategically to line up with her Special Skill's cooldown to ensure you aren't wasting damage windows.
*   **Switch Skill (Priority: Low):** This skill is less impactful for her overall DPS rotation and can be left at lower levels until your other skills are maxed.

## Awakening Progression

While Esther is functional at Zero Awakening (A0), her playstyle changes significantly as you unlock her duplicates.

*   **Awakening 1:** Provides the "Cloud" mechanic upon switching to her. This allows you to immediately begin high-damage combos without waiting for skill cooldowns.
*   **Awakening 2:** Increases the size of her attack area. This is a massive quality-of-life improvement, allowing her to hit multiple enemies and making her positioning much more forgiving.
*   **Awakening 6:** The ultimate goal for high-end players. This provides a massive multiplier to her Basic Attack, cementing her role as the premier sustained DPS in the game.

## Combat Mechanics and Rotation

To master the **esther best build**, you must understand the "Dash-Cancel" combo. Esther’s fourth hit in her basic attack string has a slight recovery animation. By dashing immediately after the fourth hit, you can reset her animation and begin the next combo instantly.

**The Ideal Rotation:**
1.  **Switch-In:** Triggers the Awakening 1 Cloud.
2.  **Basic Combo (1-2-3-4) -> Dash:** Repeat this twice.
3.  **Special Skill (E):** Refreshes the Cloud and triggers weapon/set buffs.
4.  **Basic Combo (1-2-3-4) -> Dash:** Repeat this four times.
5.  **Ultimate Skill:** Use when the boss's Suppression bar is nearly empty to maximize the knockdown window.

Following this rotation ensures that Esther always has a Dust Cloud active, which is essential for her damage scaling. If you use your Ultimate while your Special Skill is still on a long cooldown, you may find yourself with a "dead" window where your damage drops significantly.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I run Esther without her signature weapon?**

A: You can, but it is not recommended for endgame content. Her signature weapon provides a 35% Wind Damage buff that is currently unmatched by any other generic weapon in the game. If you must use a substitute, focus on weapons with high Crit Rate or Attack% substats.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm Esther's gear?**

A: The Conquest game mode is the primary source. You can start farming the necessary materials at Level 10, but the best drop rates for completed armor pieces occur at Level 13 and above. For the absolute **esther best build**, you will eventually need to farm Level 50 Conquest for the highest tier of gear.

**Q: Why is Suppression Damage so important for Esther?**

A: Esther is an Assassin class character. Her passive abilities are designed to deal significantly more damage to enemies that are in a "knocked down" or suppressed state. By stacking Suppression Damage Boost, you amplify this natural advantage, allowing her to delete boss health bars during their vulnerability phases.

**Q: Is Esther good for F2P players?**

A: Yes, Esther is very F2P friendly because her primary damage comes from her basic attacks, which are free to use. While her signature weapon and A2 awakening are helpful, a well-built A0 Esther with farmed Conquest gear can still clear most of the game's difficult content.
