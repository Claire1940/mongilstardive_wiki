## Mastering the Tag-Team Combat System

The combat in Mongil Star Dive is a dynamic, three-character tag-team system. Unlike traditional turn-based RPGs, this game requires real-time switching to maintain momentum and exploit enemy vulnerabilities.

### Elemental Weaknesses and Staggering
Every enemy in Banna belongs to an elemental class. Exploiting these weaknesses isn't just about extra damage; it is the fastest way to fill the **Stagger Gauge**. Once an enemy is staggered, they take significantly more damage and are unable to retaliate.

1.  **Identify Weakness:** Look at the icon above the enemy health bar.
2.  **Switch Character:** Tag in the hero with the elemental advantage.
3.  **Build Stagger:** Use basic attacks and skills to break their guard.
4.  **Trigger Burst:** Once staggered, use a high-damage skill (like Nienna’s) to enter Burst Mode.

### Synergistic Team Building
Creating a "monoelement" team is a popular strategy in the current meta. For example, pairing **Esther** and **Chiwan** creates a powerful Wind-element synergy that can shred through Earth-aligned bosses. However, a balanced team consisting of a Fighter, a Support, and a Tank/Utility unit is generally safer for general exploration.
