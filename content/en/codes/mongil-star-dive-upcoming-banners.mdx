## Understanding the Mongil Star Dive Summoning System

Before diving into the specifics of the **mongil star dive upcoming banners**, it is vital to understand the mechanics of the "Compass" system. Unlike many other gacha games that use a single currency for all character summons, Mongil Star Dive differentiates between standard pulls and premium, limited-time offerings.

| Item Name | Purpose | Rarity/Value |
|---|---|---|
| **Compass of Destiny** | Used for Limited/Rate-up Banners | **Premium** |
| **Compass of Promise** | Used for Standard/Permanent Banners | **Standard** |
| **Purple Upgrade Stone** | Material for Choice Grade upgrades | **Rare Utility** |

> **💡 Tip:** Always save your Compass of Destiny for limited-time banners rather than using them on standard pools. The pity system on limited banners often provides better long-term value for rare character acquisitions.
