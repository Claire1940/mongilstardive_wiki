## Gear and Stat Priorities for Gabi Builds

To get the most out of your **gabi build mongil**, you must focus on stats that enhance her "buildup" mechanics. Unlike Wind DPS units that favor pure crit rate, Earth units often benefit from increased affliction duration and resonance damage modifiers.

1. **Earth Damage Bonus**: This is a non-negotiable stat for Gabi. It scales all of her active skills and resonance procs.
2. **Neutralization Damage**: Essential for late-game Conquest mode. This allows Gabi to contribute to breaking the boss's guard.
3. **Crit Damage**: While Gabi isn't a pure crit-fisher, having high crit damage ensures her explosive resonance windows hit for maximum values.
4. **Health/Defense**: As a buildup character, Gabi spends more time on the field than typical assassins. Ensure she has enough survivability to withstand boss AOE attacks.

> **Warning:** Avoid over-investing in raw Attack stats if your Affliction Duration is low. Gabi needs the target to stay afflicted to reach her maximum damage potential.

## Advanced Combat Rotations and Synergy

Refining a **gabi build mongil** isn't just about gear; it's about the rotation. The standard meta-rotation in [Mongil Star Dive](https://www.google.com/search?q=Mongil+Star+Dive+official+site) follows a specific flow to ensure buffs are active before the main DPS hits the field.

### The Supporter-Assassin-Fighter Loop
1. **Start with Francis (Supporter)**: Use her skills to gather enemies and apply the Earth affliction debuff.
2. **Swap to Assassin**: If you are running a hybrid team, swap to your Assassin to shred defense or apply secondary debuffs.
3. **Bring in Gabi (Fighter/DPS)**: Once the enemy is grouped and debuffed, Gabi enters the field. Activate her resonance skill immediately to capitalize on the existing afflictions.
4. **Trigger Neutralization**: Use Ellie or Gabi's heavy attacks to break the stagger gauge, leading to a massive damage window.

This loop ensures that Gabi is never attacking a "dry" target. By the time she enters the fray, the enemies should already be weakened and primed for Earth-based explosions.
