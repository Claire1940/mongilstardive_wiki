## The Core Mechanics of an Esther Build

Esther functions as a Main DPS with a heavy focus on the Wind element. Her primary utility comes from her ability to apply Wind Affliction, a status effect that increases the damage enemies take from subsequent wind-based attacks. In the current 2026 meta, elemental purity is often rewarded more than hybrid teams because of how these afflictions stack and interact with support skills.

When constructing an **esther build**, you must prioritize her role as the primary "on-field" presence. Unlike sub-DPS characters who swap in for a quick burst, Esther thrives when she has maximum uptime to cycle through her rotations.

### Recommended Team Synergies

Because Mongil Star Dive limits teams to three characters, every slot is precious. You cannot afford to run redundant roles. For Esther, the goal is to surround her with characters who either enhance her Wind damage or provide crowd control (CC) to keep her safe while she executes her combos.

| Team Slot | Character | Element | Role | Why They Fit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Main DPS** | **Esther** | Wind | Primary Carry | Core of the build; applies Wind Affliction. |
| **Support** | **Chiwon** | Wind | Buffer | Provides massive Wind-specific buffs and energy. |
| **Sub-DPS/Utility**| **Sangun** | Wind | Affliction Support | Helps maintain 100% uptime on Wind debuffs. |
| **Alternative** | **Nara** | Ice | CC/Support | Top-tier CC; bubbles provide off-field damage. |

> **💡 Tip:** If you selected Chiwon from your beginner selector banner, pulling for Esther is highly recommended. Their synergy creates the strongest Wind core currently available in the game.

## Skill Breakdown and Affliction Management

The secret to a high-tier **esther build** lies in "Affliction Uptime." In Mongil Star Dive, applying an element consistently leads to a "Break" state or increased damage multipliers. Esther’s kit is designed to exploit the Wind Affliction status. 

Her skills focus on multi-hit strikes that quickly build up the affliction meter. Once the meter is full, the enemy enters a vulnerable state where wind damage is amplified. If you pair her with Sangun, who also contributes to the wind meter, you can effectively keep bosses in a perpetual state of vulnerability.

### Stat Priority for Gear

While specific gear sets may vary as new content drops throughout 2026, the fundamental stats for a DPS **esther build** remain consistent. You want to balance your critical hit rate with elemental damage bonuses.

| Stat Type | Priority | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Wind DMG %** | **Highest** | Directly multiplies all of Esther's skill output. |
| **Crit Rate** | **High** | Essential for consistent damage during burst windows. |
| **ATK %** | **Medium** | Increases the baseline for all damage calculations. |
| **Energy Regen** | **Low/Situational** | Helpful if you find her Ultimate has too much downtime. |

## Summoning Strategy: To Pull or Skip?

A common question among the community is whether to invest in an **esther build** now or save for upcoming banners like Mina or Nara. Both Mina (Fire DPS) and Nara (Ice Support) were legendary in the beta tests and are expected to arrive shortly after the launch window.

### The Wind Team vs. The Fire Team
If you already have Flare, a top-tier Fire boss killer, you might be tempted to skip Esther and wait for Mina and Nara to complete a Fire/Ice hybrid team. Nara is widely considered a "T0" support because her bubbles can be popped by fire damage for explosive results. 

However, if your account is currently lacking a dedicated carry, Esther is a safe investment. The pity system in Mongil Star Dive is quite player-friendly, allowing you to plan your pulls with precision.

| Feature | Mechanic Detail |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not pull on the limited banner if you are only 10 pulls away from pity and want a future character. The carryover system means your guaranteed pull is best saved for a character that fits your long-term team goals.

## Advanced Tactics for Esther Users

To truly master your **esther build**, you need to practice your "Switch Skills." When you swap Esther into the fight, she performs a tag-in attack that often applies a significant chunk of Wind Affliction. 

1. **Setup with Support:** Use Chiwon to drop buffs and group enemies.
2. **Tag-in Esther:** Use her switch skill to enter the field with an immediate damage boost.
3. **Burst Rotation:** Cycle through her skills to trigger the Wind Affliction pop.
4. **Ult Timing:** Save her Ultimate for when the enemy is "Broken" or has the maximum stacks of affliction for a massive finishing blow.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Esther better than Flare for boss fights?**

A: It depends on the boss's elemental weakness. Flare is currently the premier Fire boss DPS, while Esther excels in Wind-weak scenarios. Flare typically has higher single-target burst, while Esther offers better sustained damage and affliction application.

**Q: Can I run an Esther build without Chiwon?**

A: Yes, but her effectiveness will drop. You can substitute Chiwon with 4-star supports who provide general ATK buffs, but you will miss out on the specific Wind DMG multipliers that make the Esther/Chiwon duo so potent.

**Q: Does the pity carry over if I don't get Esther?**

A: Yes, if you pull on Esther's banner and do not reach a 5-star, those pulls count toward the pity on the next limited character banner (such as Mina or Nara).

**Q: What is the most important stat for an Esther build?**

A: Wind DMG % is the most critical stat. Since almost all of her damage is elemental, increasing this multiplier provides a larger overall boost than raw ATK % in most gear configurations.
