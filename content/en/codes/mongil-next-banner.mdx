## Understanding the Pity and Transfer System

One of the most player-friendly aspects of *Mongil Star Dive* is the lack of a 50/50 system. In many gacha games, reaching pity only gives you a 50% chance of getting the featured character. Here, the system is built on **Pity Transfer** and **Rate Up Bonuses**.

### How Pity Works in Mongil
1. **Soft Pity:** At 75 pulls, the "Rate Up" bonus kicks in, significantly increasing your chances of landing the featured 5-star unit.
2. **Guaranteed Acquisition:** There is a hard cap where the character is guaranteed.
3. **Pity Transfer:** This is the most important part for those eyeing the **mongil next banner**. If you pull 50 times on Esther's banner and don't get a 5-star, those 50 pulls carry over directly to Mina's banner.

You are never "wasting" summons. Even if you throw 20 pulls into a banner just to test your luck, those counts are permanently etched into your account progress for the next event. This removes the "pity bait" anxiety found in other titles.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The only way to "waste" pity is by accidentally pulling a character you didn't want. If you are close to the 75-pull soft pity mark and don't want the current featured unit, stop pulling immediately and wait for the next rotation.
