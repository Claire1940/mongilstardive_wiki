## Optimization Tips for Low-End Devices

If your device barely meets the **mongil star dive system requirements mobile**, you don't have to miss out on the story of Cloud and his terrifying nurse ally. Follow these steps to improve your frame rate:

1.  **Lower Shadow Quality:** Shadows are the most taxing setting in Mongil: Star Dive. Reducing this to "Low" can provide a 15-20% FPS boost.
2.  **Disable Monsterling Visuals:** While it's fun to see your trinkets, disabling the high-detail models for equipped Monsterlings can save memory.
3.  **Cap Frame Rate at 30 FPS:** While 60 FPS is ideal for Action RPGs, a stable 30 FPS is better than a stuttering 60 FPS during boss Conquests.
4.  **Clear Cache Regularly:** The game's codex and story logs can accumulate temporary data. Use the in-game "Clean Cache" tool in the settings menu.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the "Auto-Clear" feature for domains and boss fights once you have beaten them. This allows you to farm materials and Monsterlings without putting unnecessary thermal stress on your phone during long grinding sessions.

**Q: How do I change the game difficulty if it's lagging?**
