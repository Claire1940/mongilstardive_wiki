## Understanding Gabi: The Dokkaebi of Reckless Heart

Gabi is not your typical frontline brawler. In the lore of *Star Dive*, she is a Dokkaebi—a mythical creature often feared by humans. This makes her an excellent choice for players who prefer a "bruiser" archetype—someone who can take a hit and deal back twice the punishment.

Gabi's kit is centered around physical destruction and emotional outbursts. Her primary goal is to "smash everything" in her path while maintaining enough durability to survive the counterattack. Below is a breakdown of her core abilities as of the 2026 launch version.

| Skill Name | Description | Type | Cooldown |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Reckless Smash** | Gabi slams her weapon down, dealing massive AoE physical damage and slowing enemies. | Active | 8 Seconds |
| **Dokkaebi Roar** | A powerful shout that knocks back nearby enemies and grants Gabi a defense buff. | Utility | 15 Seconds |
| **Heart of Protection** | Gabi enters a state of focus, reducing incoming damage and increasing her attack speed. | Buff | 20 Seconds |

> **💡 Tip:** Use "Dokkaebi Roar" specifically when surrounded by small mobs to create breathing room for a fully charged "Reckless Smash."

## Advanced Strategies for Mastering Gabi

To truly excel with **star dive gabi**, you must learn the "Smash-Cancel" technique. This involves using a movement command or a utility skill immediately after the damage frame of "Reckless Smash" connects. This allows you to bypass the lengthy recovery animation, letting Gabi stay mobile even while performing heavy attacks.

Furthermore, managing her "Embrace the Nature" ultimate is key. Do not use it as soon as it is available. Instead, wait for boss phases where the enemy is stationary or staggered. The wind-up for her ultimate is significant, and missing it can result in a massive loss of DPS.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Star Dive Gabi a tank or a DPS character?**

A: Gabi is best classified as a "Bruiser" or "Off-Tank." While she has high health and defensive buffs, her primary role is to deal massive physical AoE damage. She can lead the charge, but she usually requires a dedicated healer for long encounters.

**Q: How do I unlock Gabi's "true nature" form?**

A: Her true nature is represented by her Ultimate ability, "Embrace the Nature." In 2026, there are rumors of an "Awakening" system being added in future patches that may allow for a permanent transformation, but currently, it is a temporary burst state.

**Q: What is the best weapon type for Gabi?**

A: Gabi exclusively uses heavy blunt weapons, such as clubs or maces. Look for weapons with the "Impact" trait, which increases the stagger bar damage dealt to bosses.

**Q: Can I play Star Dive Gabi effectively in PvP?**

A: Yes, Gabi is excellent in PvP for disrupting enemy formations. Her "Dokkaebi Roar" can interrupt spellcasters, and her high burst damage can catch squishy targets off guard. However, be careful of being "kited" by ranged attackers, as her mobility is limited compared to assassins.
