## Understanding Flare’s Role as a Destroyer

Flare is categorized as a Destroyer, a class specifically designed to handle "Neutralization" damage. In Mongil Star Dive, bosses possess a stagger bar that must be depleted before they can be effectively damaged. Flare excels at this, acting as the vanguard for your team. Her ultimate skill is particularly potent because it applies a Fire Affliction. This debuff does more than just deal damage over time; it actively decreases the enemy's Fire Resistance, making her an indispensable core for any mono-fire or hybrid team.

## The Four Damage Pillars of Mongil Star Dive

To truly optimize a **flare build mongil star dive** composition, you must understand how the game calculates bonus damage. Each class in the game specializes in one of these four categories. Flare, as a Destroyer, focuses on the second pillar:

1.  **Brawl Damage:** Extra damage based on the number of enemies and teammates nearby. This is the specialty of the Fighter class.
2.  **Neutralization Damage:** The efficiency at which you stagger and "break" a boss. This is Flare’s primary job.
3.  **Suppression Damage:** Increased damage dealt to enemies that are currently CC'd (Crowd Controlled) or in a broken state. Assassins capitalize on this.
4.  **Support Damage:** Bonus damage triggered by tag-skills and basic attacks from supports.

By pairing Flare with an Assassin, you create a "Break and Burst" loop. Flare breaks the boss (Neutralization), and the Assassin follows up with massive burst damage (Suppression).

## Progression Strategy for New Players

If you are just starting your journey in 2026, the **flare build mongil star dive** path is highly recommended because Flare is often available through early banners or selector tickets. 

1.  **Commit Early:** Choose your core three characters and stick with them until at least level 50.
2.  **Focus on the Break:** In boss fights, don't waste your Assassin's high-cooldown skills while the boss's shield is up. Switch to Flare, use her Neutralization skills, and only swap back to your DPS once the "Broken" status appears.
3.  **Support Synergy:** If you find yourself dying too often, swap your secondary DPS for a second support like Francis or Ellie. Flare provides enough utility that a "two-support, one-destroyer" team can slowly but surely clear even the hardest content.

> **💡 Strategy Tip:** Use Flare's ultimate as soon as it is available. The Fire Resistance shred lasts long enough to cover multiple character swaps, allowing your entire team to benefit from the elemental weakness.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Flare better than other Destroyers like Cloud?**

A: Currently, yes. Flare’s ability to shred elemental resistance via her ultimate gives her a higher utility ceiling than Cloud, who focuses more on pure physical stagger. Flare fits into more team archetypes in the 2026 meta.

**Q: What is the most important stat for a flare build mongil star dive?**

A: Neutralization Damage and Proficiency are your top priorities. You want Flare to break shields as quickly as possible so your Assassins can trigger their Suppression damage bonuses.

**Q: Can I run Flare in a non-fire team?**

A: Absolutely. While she shines in fire teams, her shield-breaking capabilities are universal. She is frequently used alongside Wind characters like Esther because Flare is simply the most efficient Destroyer currently available.

**Q: Which support works best with Flare for free-to-play players?**

A: Ellie is the best F2P choice. Her snacks provide healing, and her sugar charges buff the team's stagger potential, which directly complements Flare’s role as a Destroyer.
