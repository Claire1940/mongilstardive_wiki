## Flare: The Premier Fire Destroyer

Flare is classified as a Destroyer, a role specifically designed to handle "Neutralization" damage. In Mongil Star Dive, Neutralization refers to the stagger damage dealt to a boss's break bar. Once this bar is depleted, the boss enters a staggered state, taking significantly increased damage from your entire party.

Flare’s kit is built around maximizing this window. Her ultimate skill is particularly potent because it applies a Fire Affliction debuff. This does not just deal Fire damage over time; it actively decreases the target's Fire Resistance. This makes her the cornerstone of any fire-based elemental team, as she effectively "softens" the target for her allies.

## Understanding Damage Mechanics in 2026

To truly optimize a **mongil star dive flare build**, you must understand the four primary damage boosts that govern combat. Each class role in the game specializes in one of these four areas.

1.  **Brawl Damage:** This is extra damage dealt when multiple enemies and teammates are nearby. This is the specialty of the Fighter class, making them excellent for wave clearing.
2.  **Neutralization:** This is the stagger damage used to break bosses. As a Destroyer, Flare excels here, allowing your team to bypass boss mechanics faster.
3.  **Suppression Damage:** This is the bonus damage dealt to enemies that are CC'd or staggered. This is where Assassins like Esther provide their value.
4.  **Support Damage:** This increases the damage of basic attacks and tag-in skills. Supports utilize this to keep the team's DPS high even while rotating characters.

## Best Team Compositions for Flare

While Flare is strong individually, she reaches her full potential when paired with characters that can capitalize on her fire resistance shred or provide the necessary sustain to keep her on the field.

### 1. The Mono-Fire Powerhouse
This team focuses on stacking Fire Afflictions to melt enemies. By running multiple fire units, you benefit from the cumulative resistance reduction provided by Flare.

*   **Main Destroyer:** Flare
*   **Secondary DPS/Fighter:** Angel (Provides Brawl damage for wave clearing)
*   **Support:** Francis or Ellie

Ellie is a particularly interesting choice for a **mongil star dive flare build**. Her "Sugar Charge" mechanic allows her to deal surprisingly high stagger damage while providing heals. This complements Flare’s goal of breaking the boss as quickly as possible.

### 2. The Meta Progression Duo (Flare + Esther)
Currently, the strongest duo in the game for general progression is Flare and Esther. This "Destroyer + Assassin" combo is the gold standard for boss hunting. Flare breaks the boss (Neutralization), and Esther follows up with massive burst damage during the stagger window (Suppression).

*   **Slot 1:** Flare (The Breaker)
*   **Slot 2:** Esther (The Finisher)
*   **Slot 3:** Chiwon or Francis (The Buffer/Healer)

### 3. High-Efficiency Wind/Fire Hybrid
If you lack a full fire roster, Flare fits perfectly into wind-heavy teams. Since wind units like Sangoon or Esther are top-tier DPS, Flare acts as the universal "key" to unlock their damage by handling the break mechanics that they struggle with.

| Team Member | Role | Why they work with Flare |
|---|---|---|
| **Esther** | Assassin | Capitalizes on the break windows Flare creates. |
| **Angel** | Fighter | Handles AoE scenarios where Flare might feel slow. |
| **Ellie** | Support | Adds additional stagger damage via snacks/sugar charges. |
| **Chiwon** | Support | Provides grouping and wind-specific buffs for Esther. |

## Gear and Progression Priorities

When building Flare, your primary focus should be on **Proficiency** and **Fire Damage Bonus**. Proficiency scales her Neutralization capabilities, ensuring that she can break even the toughest late-game bosses in a single rotation.

1.  **Weapon:** Look for "Ignited Strikers" or any gear that adds flat Fire Damage.
2.  **Armor:** Focus on defensive stats, as Flare needs to stay in the fray to build her break meter.
3.  **Accessories:** Prioritize Neutralization Rate and Cooldown Reduction. The faster she can cast her ultimate, the more uptime you have on the fire resistance debuff.

> **Warning:** Avoid spreading your resources too thin. In the early 2026 game, materials are scarce. Focus on leveling Flare's ultimate and her primary Neutralization skill first.

**Q: Is Flare better than other Destroyers for a mongil star dive flare build?**
