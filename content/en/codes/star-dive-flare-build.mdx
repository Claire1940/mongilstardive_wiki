As a premier Destroyer class, Flare excels at breaking through boss defenses and applying devastating elemental debuffs that benefit the entire party. ## Flare Core Mechanics and Role

In the current 2026 meta, Flare is widely considered the best Destroyer in the game. To play her effectively, you must understand the four primary damage types: Brawl, Neutralization, Suppression, and Support damage. As a Destroyer, Flare's primary responsibility is **Neutralization**. This is the stagger damage required to "break" a boss, leaving them vulnerable to massive burst damage.

Flare is a Fire-elemental powerhouse. Her kit revolves around applying "Fire Affliction" through her ultimate skill. This debuff serves a dual purpose: it deals continuous fire damage over time and significantly reduces the enemy's fire resistance. This makes her the cornerstone of any fire-based team, as she enables her allies to deal exponentially more damage.

| Damage Type | Description | Flare's Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Neutralization** | Stagger damage used to break boss shields. | **Primary Focus** |
| **Suppression** | Extra damage dealt to CC'd or staggered enemies. | Secondary Focus |
| **Brawl Damage** | Multiplier based on the number of nearby enemies/allies. | Low Priority |
| **Support Damage** | Damage triggered by tag-ins and support skills. | Situational |

## Best Star Dive Flare Build: Skill Synergy

When building Flare, your focus should always be on her ability to trigger the "Broken" state on bosses as quickly as possible. Because resources are scarce in the early game, focusing on her active skills and ultimate is more efficient than spreading points into basic attack passives.

> **💡 Tip:** Always initiate with Flare’s ultimate before switching to your main damage dealer. The Fire Resistance reduction from her Fire Affliction is a massive force multiplier for the rest of your rotation.

Her ultimate isn't just about the raw damage numbers; it's about the utility. By lowering fire resistance, she makes characters like Angel or the upcoming Mina much more potent. If you are running her in a hybrid team, she still provides excellent value simply through her high neutralization proficiency, which is higher than almost any other character currently available.

## Progression and Resource Management

One of the most common mistakes when finalizing a **star dive flare build** is over-investing in too many characters simultaneously. Materials for leveling up proficiency and skills are limited. You should prioritize Flare's level and her weapon upgrades to ensure her Neutralization stats stay ahead of the boss's shield scaling.

1.  **Prioritize Destroyer Proficiency:** This directly increases Flare's stagger damage.
2.  **Focus on Neutralization Gear:** Look for equipment that boosts "Neutralization Proficiency" rather than raw Attack power for Flare.
3.  **Ultimate Skill Levels:** Level Flare's ultimate to increase the duration and potency of the Fire Affliction debuff.
4.  **Save for Future Banners:** Characters like Nara (Ice/Fire support) and Mina (Fire DPS) will eventually replace the free-to-play options in Flare's ideal team.

> **Warning:** Avoid spending your rare upgrade stones on 3-star gear. Wait until you acquire 4-star or higher equipment with Fire Damage or Neutralization sub-stats.

## Elemental Afflictions and Boss Breaking

In *Star Dive*, the interaction between elements is crucial. Flare’s ability to apply Fire Affliction is not just a damage boost; it’s a strategic tool. When an enemy is afflicted with Fire, they take more damage from all fire sources. If you pair Flare with another fire unit, the synergy is multiplicative. 

However, even in non-fire teams, Flare remains relevant because of the way "Suppression Damage" works. Once Flare breaks a boss using her high Neutralization stats, the boss enters a "Suppressed" state. During this time, your Assassin class characters (like Esther or Penny) deal significantly increased damage. This Destroyer-Assassin combo is the foundation of high-level play.

## Advanced Tactics for Flare

To truly master Flare, you need to time your tag-ins. You should stay on Flare for approximately 70-80% of the shield-breaking phase. Once the boss's white bar is nearly empty, prepare your rotation:
1.  **Use Flare's Special Skill** to deal a final burst of stagger.
2.  **Cast Flare's Ultimate** to apply the Fire Affliction debuff right as the shield breaks.
3.  **Immediately Tag-in your Assassin.**
4.  **Unleash the Assassin's burst combo** while the boss is suppressed and fire resistance is lowered.

| Strategy Phase | Action | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Neutralization Phase** | Use Flare's basic attacks and snacks (if using Ellie). | Deplete the boss shield bar. |
| **Break Point** | Activate Flare Ultimate Skill. | Apply Fire Affliction and Res Down. |
| **Suppression Phase** | Switch to Assassin (Esther/Angel/Mina). | Maximize burst damage during the stagger. |

## FAQ

**Q: Is Flare better than other Destroyers for general content?**

A: Yes, Flare is currently the most versatile Destroyer due to her high base Neutralization proficiency and the utility of her Fire Affliction debuff, which benefits both her and her teammates.

**Q: Should I run a mono-fire team or a hybrid team with Flare?**

A: For early progression, a hybrid team (e.g., Flare, Esther, Francis) is usually better as it covers more elemental weaknesses. For end-game bosses specifically weak to fire, a mono-fire team becomes superior.

**Q: What is the most important stat for a star dive flare build?**

A: **Neutralization Proficiency** is the most important stat. It dictates how quickly you can break boss shields, which is Flare's primary job in any team composition.

**Q: Which support character works best with Flare?**

A: Ellie is an excellent free-to-play choice because she provides healing and increases neutralization damage. Francis is a better pure healer, while the upcoming Nara will be her best-in-slot support.
