## Optimization Tips for Low-End Devices

If your device barely meets the **mongil star dive mobile requirements**, you can still enjoy the game by making specific adjustments in the settings menu. The "cultured" character designs and detailed environments can be scaled down to prioritize frame rate over visual fidelity.

*   **Resolution Scaling:** Drop the resolution to "Medium" or "Standard." This is the single most effective way to reduce GPU load.
*   **Shadow Quality:** Set shadows to "Low." Real-time shadows in the open map zones consume significant processing power.
*   **Anti-Aliasing:** Disable AA or set it to FXAA to reduce the blurriness without taxing the hardware.
*   **Monsterling Visibility:** In the settings, you can toggle the visual complexity of the Monsterling trinkets to reduce the number of rendered polygons on your character.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the "Auto-Clear" feature for domains and boss fights once you have beaten them. This allows you to farm materials without putting your phone through the stress of a full 3D combat encounter.
