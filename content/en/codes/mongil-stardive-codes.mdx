## Combat Mechanics: The Tag-Team System

Mongil: Star Dive separates itself from other gacha RPGs through its real-time three-character tag system. Instead of having all characters on screen at once, you control one "Active" hero while the other two remain in reserve. 

When you swap characters, the incoming hero often performs a powerful **Switch-In Skill**. Using the diamonds and gold from **mongil stardive codes** allows you to level up these skills across your entire roster. This is crucial because hitting an enemy's weak point often requires a specific elemental type that your current active character might not possess.
