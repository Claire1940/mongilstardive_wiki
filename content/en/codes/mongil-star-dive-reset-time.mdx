## FAQ

**Q: What exactly is the mongil star dive reset time for the Global server?**

A: The Global server typically resets at 00:00 UTC. Depending on your local time zone, this usually falls in the late evening for North American players or early morning for European players.

**Q: Can I skip the tutorial to speed up my account reset?**

A: You cannot skip the entire tutorial, but you can skip all cutscenes and approximately 50% of the combat encounters by rushing to the objective markers. You must reach the "Monster Taming" quest to unlock summons.

**Q: Is it worth rerolling for limited banners?**

A: It is generally difficult to reroll for limited banners because most limited pulls are tied to event quests that unlock later in the game. It is more efficient to reroll on the standard or "Eternal Promise" banner to secure a strong foundation.

**Q: What happens if I miss the daily reset time?**

A: If you miss the **mongil star dive reset time**, you lose the opportunity to claim that day's daily mission rewards and dungeon entries. These do not stack, so it is vital to log in and at least complete the quick-clear missions daily.
