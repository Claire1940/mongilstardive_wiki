## Reroll Guide and Account Optimization

Rerolling in Mongil: Star Dive is more challenging than in other Netmarble titles. The "salting" method (adding +1 to your email address) does not work with the current account system. To reroll for a better start, you must create a completely new email account for each attempt.

### Recommended Reroll Steps
*   Complete the tutorial until you unlock the mail system.
*   Collect all pre-registration rewards and launch gift codes.
*   Spend your first 20 pulls on the **Eternal Promise** banner.
*   Aim for **Flare** as your guaranteed 5-star.
*   Use any remaining currency on the Aster banner.
*   If you fail to get at least two 5-star units (Flare + one other), reset the account.

> **💡 Tip:** Check the Netmarble Support Program for Mongil: Star Dive. Linking your account to an official content creator can often net you extra codes for free summons.

## Resource Management for 2026

As a gacha game, Mongil: Star Dive thrives on "resource fatigue." While the game provides many summons during the first week, these rewards dry up significantly once the initial missions are cleared. To prepare for the **mongil star dive next banner**, follow these management rules:

*   **Don't pull on Standard:** Never use premium currency on the standard banner. Only use free tickets earned through gameplay.
*   **Focus on Destroyers:** Bosses in the Conquest mode have 5-minute timers. Without a Destroyer like Flare, you will likely fail the damage check even with high-level DPS units.
*   **Save for Mina:** If you already have Flare and a decent AoE unit like Angel or Cloud, stop summoning immediately and save everything for Mina’s arrival in three weeks.
