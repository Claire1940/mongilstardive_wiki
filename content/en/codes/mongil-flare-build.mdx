## Understanding Flare: The Fire Ranged Destroyer

In Mongil: Star Dive, characters are divided into specific classes that dictate their proficiency and scaling. Flare belongs to the Destroyer class, which is specifically designed to excel in boss battles. While Fighters thrive in melee brawls and Assassins hunt for burst openings, the Destroyer is the king of the stagger bar.

A **mongil flare build** scales primarily off of Destroyer Proficiency. This stat increases Neutralization damage, which is the specific damage type required to deplete the white stagger bar beneath a boss's health. Once this bar is broken, the boss enters a vulnerable state, allowing your entire team to deal massive damage.

| Class Attribute | Primary Scaling Stat | Combat Role | Target Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Destroyer** | Destroyer Proficiency | Hybrid DPS/Stagger | Bosses & Elites |
| **Fighter** | Fighter Proficiency | Melee Sustained DPS | Grouped Enemies |
| **Assassin** | Assassin Proficiency | Burst Damage | High-Value Targets |
| **Support** | Support Proficiency | Buffs/Healing | Team Survival |

> **💡 Tip:** Always check your gear for "Destroyer Proficiency" sub-stats. For Flare, this is more valuable than raw attack power when fighting bosses because it accelerates the break phase.

## Essential Skills and Fire Affliction

The core of a powerful **mongil flare build** lies in her ability to apply Fire Affliction and Dragon Marks. Flare isn't just a solo damage dealer; she is a force multiplier for all fire-elemental units. Her passive traits allow her to reduce the fire resistance of enemies, which directly increases the damage output of her fire-based skills and those of her allies.

When Flare lands her special attacks, she often triggers Fire Affliction. This status effect does more than just damage over time; it lowers the enemy's elemental defenses. When building Flare, you want to focus on maintaining 100% uptime on these debuffs.

### Key Skill Interactions
1.  **Fire Resistance Down:** Lowers the enemy's defense against fire attacks by a percentage.
2.  **Dragon Mark:** Enhances Flare's own damage against the marked target.
3.  **Neutralization Strike:** Deals massive stagger damage when the enemy is preparing a heavy attack.

## The Best Fire Mono-Team Synergy

To get the most out of your **mongil flare build**, you should transition away from multi-elemental teams as soon as possible. In the current 2026 meta, monochromatic (mono) teams provide the highest damage ceiling because of elemental resistance shredding.

Flare shines brightest when paired with Leho, a Fire Assassin. Leho provides a massive 15% fire resistance reduction through his ultimate, which stacks beautifully with Flare’s own debuffs. This synergy allows the team to "melt" bosses that would otherwise be resistant to physical damage.

| Team Slot | Recommended Character | Role in Strategy |

| **Destroyer** | **Flare** | Staggering, Ranged Pressure, Fire Shred |
| **Assassin** | **Leho** | Main DPS, Fire Resistance Reduction |

For the third slot, if you are tackling endgame content, a Support is mandatory. Healing and survival buffs are necessary because enemies in 2026 hit significantly harder than in previous patches. However, if you are speed-running lower-level content, replacing the Support with another Fire DPS can further optimize your **mongil flare build**.

## Gear and Stat Priorities for Flare

When equipping your **mongil flare build**, you must balance offensive stats with class-specific proficiency. Many players make the mistake of stacking only Attack (ATK), but for a Destroyer, this leads to longer boss fights because the stagger phase takes too long to trigger.

| Gear Slot | Preferred Main Stat | Ideal Sub-Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Armor** | **HP / Defense** | Destroyer Proficiency, HP % |

| **Accessory** | **Crit Damage** | Fire Resistance Shred, ATK % |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not ignore the Stamina bar. If you run out of Stamina, Flare cannot perform dodges or special counters, which are vital for maintaining her ranged advantage.

## Combat Mechanics: Mastering the Stagger

The most critical aspect of playing a **mongil flare build** is mastering the "Break" mechanic. Every boss has a white bar under their health. As a Destroyer, Flare's primary job is to delete this bar.

1.  **Initial Setup:** Start the fight with your Support to apply any defensive buffs.
2.  **Debuff Application:** Switch to Flare and use her ranged skills to apply Fire Affliction and Dragon Marks.
3.  **The Break Phase:** Use Flare's heavy neutralization skills when the boss displays the yellow diamond icon. This indicates a counter-opportunity.
4.  **The All-Out Attack:** Once the stagger bar is empty, the boss will be stunned. Switch to your Assassin (like Leho) and unleash all ultimates.

By saving your ultimates for the stagger phase, you ensure that every hit benefits from the increased damage multiplier that occurs when a boss is broken. This is the cornerstone of the **mongil flare build** strategy.

## Link Chains and Monster Links

Link Chains are a unique mechanic in Mongil: Star Dive that allow you to summon captured monsters for assist attacks. For a **mongil flare build**, you should look for Monster Links that trigger on "Switch Skills" or "Fire Damage."

The **Taglock** monster link is a popular choice for Flare. When you switch Flare into the battle, Taglock will jump in and strike the enemy, dealing physical damage and potentially rendering them airborne. This creates a safe window for Flare to begin her ranged fire rotations.

### How to Trigger Link Chains
*   **Equip the Monster Link:** Ensure the monster is slotted into Flare’s link menu.
*   **Meet the Condition:** Most links require a "Switch Skill" (switching characters) or an "Evasion Counter."
*   **Follow-Up:** Use the opening created by the Link Chain to apply Flare’s Dragon Mark.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Flare better than a melee Destroyer?**

A: Flare’s advantage is her range. While melee Destroyers often have to dodge more frequently, a **mongil flare build** allows you to maintain consistent stagger damage from a safe distance, which is often more efficient for complex boss mechanics.

**Q: What is the most important stat for a mongil flare build?**

A: Destroyer Proficiency is the most important for her role as a boss-breaker. However, you still need Fire Damage % and Crit stats to ensure she contributes meaningful DPS during the stagger phase.

**Q: Can I use Flare in a non-fire team?**

A: Yes, she is a solid Destroyer in any team, but she loses about 30-40% of her potential effectiveness if she isn't paired with other Fire units who can take advantage of her fire resistance shredding.

**Q: How do I trigger Flare's special counter?**

A: Wait for the yellow diamond icon to appear on the enemy. Perform a perfect dodge, then immediately press the attack button to execute a counter-animation that deals high neutralization damage.
