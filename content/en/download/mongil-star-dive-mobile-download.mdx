As a Monster Tamer, you can bind these monsters to your service. Once bound, they enter a "linked" state with their owner. This allows them to transform into their compact Monsterling forms at your command, making them easy to transport and manage.

## Combat Mechanics: Triple Tag Team Action

The combat in Mongil: Star Dive is fast-paced and intuitive. Unlike traditional turn-based monster games, this is a real-time action RPG. You take a party of three characters into battle, but you only control one at a time. The "Triple Tag" system allows you to swap between your party members instantly.

When you switch characters, you trigger a "Tag Skill." These are powerful abilities that can interrupt enemy attacks or provide massive buffs to your incoming character. Success in high-level dungeons depends on your ability to chain these Tag Skills together to create devastating combos.

### Character Spotlight: Cloud and Verna
Cloud and Verna are the primary protagonists you meet early on. Cloud is a balanced physical attacker, while Verna provides essential support and magical utility. Alongside their mysterious cat companion, Nyanners, they represent the core of the Monster Tamers guild.
