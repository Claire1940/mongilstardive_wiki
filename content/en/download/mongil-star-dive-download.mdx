### The Monsterling Mechanic
In this game, monsters are defined by the "magic stone" they carry. When a monster is defeated or exhausted, it doesn't truly die as long as its **Monsterling**—its magical essence core—remains intact. You can bind these Monsterlings to your characters, allowing them to transform and fight by your side. Interestingly, pouring hot water onto a Monsterling is a unique lore-friendly way to force them back into their original forms!
