A unique feature that sets this game apart is the concept of "Monsterlings." This is the magical essence and core of a monster. Even if a monster appears defeated, as long as its Monsterling remains intact, it can be revived.

*   **Transformation:** Bound monsters can transform into their compact Monsterling forms at the owner's command for easy transport.
