### The Origin of Monsters and Monsterlings
In Belana, monsters are defined by the presence of a "magic stone" within their bodies. While they are biologically similar to other creatures, this stone grants them supernatural abilities. A key mechanic you will encounter after your **mongil stardive download** is the concept of **Monsterlings**.

When a monster's physical form is exhausted, it reverts to its core magical essence—the Monsterling. As long as this core remains intact, the monster is not truly dead. As a Tamer, you can bind these Monsterlings to yourself, allowing you to summon them in battle or keep them in a "linked" state for passive bonuses.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** If you find a Monsterling in the wild, pouring hot water on it can cause it to revert to its original form without an owner's command, though this can be dangerous if the monster is hostile!
