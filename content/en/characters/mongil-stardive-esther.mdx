## Skills and Combat Mechanics

The core of the **mongil stardive esther** gameplay loop is her Special Skill, "Housekeeping!" This skill creates a zone that fundamentally changes how her Basic Attacks function. Understanding the interaction between her Dust Cloud and her passive traits is essential for maximizing your damage per second (DPS).

### The Dust Cloud Strategy
When Esther is standing within her Dust Cloud (created by her Special Skill), her Basic Attacks transition from standard Physical hits into rapid-fire Wind DMG strikes. Furthermore, her Passive Trait, also titled **Maid's Double Life**, grants her a 15% ATK boost as long as she remains within this field. To play her optimally, you must always ensure the enemy is trapped within the smoke screen while Esther remains inside to benefit from the stat buffs.

## Advancement and Awakening Priority

If you are lucky enough to pull multiple copies of Esther, her Awakening (Advancement) levels provide significant power spikes. In Mongil: Star Dive, these tiers unlock new mechanics rather than just raw stats.

*   **A1 (I'll Clean This Up):** Triggers a Dust Cloud even when using her Switch Skill. This is a massive quality-of-life upgrade for rotations.
*   **A2 (Broom Blade, Silver Flash):** Increases the range of the Dust Cloud and adds a 50% DMG bonus when attacking inside it. This is her most important milestone.
*   **A4 (No Troublemakers Allowed):** Grants a 50% Crit DMG boost for 10s after using her Special Skill, provided she doesn't take damage.
*   **A6 (Final Form of the Hidden Cloud Style):** Increases Enhanced Basic Attack damage by 50% and expands the hit range.

> **Warning:** Esther's A4 buff is removed if she takes damage. This makes characters with shields or high crowd control essential partners to ensure she remains untouched during her burst window.

## Top Team Compositions 2026

Esther is a selfish DPS in the sense that she needs enemies to stay in one spot. Therefore, she performs best when paired with "gatherers" or units that can trigger elemental resonance. This team focuses on maximizing Esther's personal damage by grouping enemies and debuffing their resistances.

## Gameplay Strategy & Rotation

To get the most out of **mongil stardive esther**, follow this standard combat rotation:

1.  **Initiate with Support:** Use Bonnie or Jiwon to gather enemies into a tight group.
2.  **Deploy Special Skill:** Switch to Esther and immediately use **Housekeeping!** to drop the Dust Cloud on the gathered mobs.
3.  **Burst Window:** Unleash your Enhanced Basic Attacks. Focus on completing the full 3-hit combo, as the final hit has the highest damage multiplier.
4.  **Ultimate Finisher:** If the Ultimate is available, use it while the enemies are still Confused and within the cloud to benefit from the Wind resistance shred from your Monsterlings.
5.  **Switch Out:** Once the Dust Cloud expires (6 seconds), switch back to your Sub-DPS. This triggers Esther's Switch Skill, where she will remain briefly on the field to perform an ambush slash if an enemy gets close.

## FAQ

**Q: Is mongil stardive esther viable for F2P players?**

A: Yes. While her A2 and A6 advancements are powerful, Esther is highly effective at A0 (no dupes) because her base kit includes the "Confused" status effect, which provides excellent natural survivability.

**Q: What is the best alternative if I don't have the Wind-Harboring Broom?**

A: Any 4-star artifact that increases Crit Rate or Assassin-specific ATK will work. Look for the "Shadow Assassin's Edge" as a reliable placeholder until you can farm her signature weapon.

**Q: Does Esther's Dust Cloud affect bosses?**

A: Yes, the Dust Cloud inflicts the Confused status on most bosses, though some elite bosses may have higher resistance to the "Airborne" effect from her Ultimate. The damage buff Esther receives while inside the cloud always applies regardless of the enemy type.

**Q: How does Esther compare to other Wind characters like Sangun?**

A: Sangun focuses more on immediate burst and switch-in damage, whereas Esther provides more sustained on-field presence through her enhanced basic attacks. They actually work very well together in a mono-Wind team.
