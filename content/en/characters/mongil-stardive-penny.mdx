## Core Mechanics: The Coin System

The defining feature of **mongil stardive penny** is her "Electric Charge" passive. Unlike other characters who rely purely on cooldowns, Penny must manage a physical resource on the field: Coins.

1.  **Generation:** Landing the 3rd hit of her Basic Attack (Titanium Bow) generates one Coin on the field. You can also generate a Coin by performing a successful Critical Dodge.
2.  **Collection:** Penny must physically move to pick up the Coin (unless you have her A1 Awakening, which automates this).
3.  **Consumption:** Once a Coin is held, Penny’s Special Skill (Electric Overload) becomes empowered, dealing massive AoE damage and triggering a Lightning Explosion.

## How to Optimize Penny for Conquest

In Conquest mode, bosses often have Groggy bars that need to be depleted. While Penny is a Fighter (not a Destroyer like Flare), her "Electric Overload" deals respectable Groggy damage when empowered by a Coin. 

To maximize her efficiency:
1.  Open the fight with her **Ultimate (Thunderstorm Shot)** to apply Lightning Affliction.
2.  Use **Normal Attacks** until a Coin drops.
3.  Immediately use the **Empowered Special Skill** to trigger the Lightning Bolts.
4.  Cycle back to Normal Attacks to trigger the **Spirit King** 4-set bonus.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Penny better than Flare in Mongil: Star Dive?**

A: It depends on the content. Flare is generally considered the superior "Boss Killer" due to her Destroyer class and massive burst. However, Penny offers safer ranged gameplay and superior AoE (Area of Effect) for clearing waves of enemies in World and Dimensional Rift modes.

**Q: Does Penny need her signature artifact to be viable?**

A: While "Savvy Investment" is her best option, she is perfectly viable with "Frenzied Struggle" or other ATK-boosting artifacts. The most important factor for Penny is reaching her A1 Awakening to automate Coin collection.

**Q: What is the best way to farm equipment for a mongil stardive penny build?**

A: Focus on clearing the Lightning-elemental dungeons in the Path of Trials. You are looking for the Spirit King set with primary stats focusing on Lightning DMG Boost and Crit DMG.

**Q: Why isn't my Penny doing enough damage?**

A: Check your Lightning DMG Boost stat. Because Penny's Crit Rate is tied to this specific stat via her passive, neglecting Lightning DMG will result in very few critical hits, significantly lowering her overall DPS.
