## Elemental Synergy and Combat Roles

Understanding **mongil stardive all characters** requires more than just knowing their names; you must understand how they interact with the environment. Each element has a specific role in the current meta.

### The Fire Element: Pure Aggression
Fire characters like **Mina** and **Flare** focus on high burst damage and "Burn" status effects. They are essential for clearing waves of enemies quickly and dealing massive damage to Earth-type bosses.

### The Ice Element: Control and Freeze
Characters such as **Ophelia** and **Sera** prioritize freezing enemies in place. This is vital for high-level dungeons where enemy movement speed can overwhelm your party. Ophelia, in particular, is considered a top-tier Prime unit due to her ability to lock down entire screens of monsters.

### The Lightning Element: Speed and Chain Damage
Lightning units like **Penny** and **Yeonhwa** excel at chaining damage between multiple targets. They often have high critical hit rates and can bypass certain defensive buffs, making them excellent for speed-running daily missions.

## How to Build a Balanced Team

When selecting from **mongil stardive all characters**, follow these three rules to ensure your party is ready for any challenge:

1.  **The Rule of Three Elements:** Try to carry at least three different elements in your active party. This prevents you from being "hard-countered" by a specific boss type.
2.  **Sustain is Mandatory:** Unless you are over-leveled for a dungeon, always include a support or tank like **Daisy** or **Gabi**. Pure damage teams often fail in the later stages of the 2026 campaign.
3.  **Ultimate Synergy:** Look for characters whose ultimates work together. For example, use Ophelia to freeze enemies, then swap to Flare to trigger "Melt" damage for a massive multiplier.

> **Warning:** Do not ignore "Choice" rarity characters. Units like **Bonnie** and **Verna** are often easier to upgrade (limit break) than Prime units, making them stronger in the mid-game.
