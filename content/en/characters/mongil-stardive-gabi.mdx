## Gabi: Character Profile and Base Stats

Gabi serves as the bold leader of the youkai in the Forest of Fireflies. Despite her reckless appearance, she is a guardian who maintains the delicate balance between the human and youkai realms. In gameplay terms, she is a melee Fighter who relies on heavy hits and the unique "Petrification" status effect to neutralize threats. 

Unlike pure burst assassins, Gabi focuses on a ramp-up playstyle. Her effectiveness increases as the battle progresses, making her a T0.5 choice for extended encounters. Below are her base statistics at level 1:

## Skill Kit and Mechanics Breakdown

The core of **mongil stardive gabi** lies in her ability to generate and consume Spiritual Power. This resource is not just a damage buff; it fundamentally changes how her basic attacks function, turning her from a standard brawler into a crowd-control machine.

### Passive: Dokkaebi Power
This is the engine of Gabi's kit. Landing the 4th hit of her basic attack combo grants one stack of Spiritual Power (up to a maximum of 3). Furthermore, this hit has a chance to generate a "Rock" on the battlefield. These rocks are not just obstacles; when destroyed by Physical or Earth damage, they explode, inflicting Petrification on nearby enemies and granting Gabi another stack of Spiritual Power.

### Basic Attack: Dokkaebi Bat
Gabi swings her bat wildly, dealing Physical damage. The final hit of the chain converts to Earth damage.
*   **Crushing Blow:** By holding the attack button with 3 stacks of Spiritual Power, Gabi unleashes a Crushing Blow. This move inflicts Petrification and deals massive Earth damage.
*   **Neutralization:** If you time a Crushing Blow during an enemy's special attack, you can momentarily neutralize them, effectively canceling their move and gaining immediate Spiritual Power stacks if "Slugger's Will" is active.

### Ultimate: Dokkaebi's Domain
Gabi summons a massive Wooden Guardian Statue. This is arguably the best ultimate in the current Fighter meta for Conquest mode. It deals immediate Earth damage and applies Earth Affliction. While the statue is active:
1.  Gabi's basic attacks are enhanced and deal Earth damage.
2.  Spiritual Power is granted periodically.
3.  Teammates receive Earth DMG and DEF boosts.

## Optimal Team Compositions

Gabi is an Earth-element specialist, meaning she thrives when paired with characters who can shred Earth resistance or provide buffs that scale with her high base stats.

### The Earth Dominance Team
*   **Gabi (Main DPS):** Focuses on stacking Spiritual Power and executing Crushing Blows.
*   **Francis (Support):** Francis provides essential buffs through Switch Skills that exponentially raise Gabi's attack power.
*   **Ellie (Breaker):** Ellie is used to quickly deplete the enemy's Stagger Gauge. Once the enemy is staggered, Gabi can safely execute her full combo without interruption.

### Strategic Rotation
1.  Start with **Ellie** to reduce the enemy's groggy gauge.
2.  Switch to **Francis** to apply offensive buffs.
3.  Tag in **Gabi**, using her Switch Skill "Hoorah!" to gain Slugger's Will.
4.  Execute basic attacks to spawn Rocks, destroy them for Petrification, and then unleash the Ultimate Skill once the boss is staggered.

**Q: Does Petrification work on all bosses?**
