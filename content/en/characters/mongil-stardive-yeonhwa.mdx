## Skill Breakdown and Combat Mechanics

The core of **mongil stardive yeonhwa** lies in her ability to generate Shadows. These are not just visual effects; they are functional clones that mimic her actions or provide utility. Understanding the difference between a Shadow that deals damage and a Shadow used for utility is the key to mastering her rotation.

### Basic Attack: Bloody Bamboo
Yeonhwa's basic string consists of quick slashes with her bamboo blade. While the initial hits deal Physical DMG, the final hit of the sequence is the most important. Landing the finishing blow generates a **[Shadow]** that follows her actions and deals additional Physical DMG.

### Special Skill: Thunder Chain Slash
This is her primary gap-closer. Yeonhwa quickly approaches the enemy and delivers a series of slashes dealing Lightning DMG. A unique feature of this skill is its ability to be cast a second time within a short window. However, players should be cautious, as using the follow-up strike extends the total cooldown of the ability.

### Ultimate: Storm Call
When activating Storm Call, Yeonhwa uses her blade as a lightning rod to strike nearby enemies. This deals massive Lightning DMG and launches enemies into the air (Airborne status). More importantly, she enters the **[Lightning Strike]** state.

| Effect Name | Duration | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lightning Strike** | Set Duration | All attacks deal **Lightning DMG** and apply **Lightning Affliction**. |
| **Shadow Bonus** | Active | Basic attacks generate a Shadow that deals bonus DMG based on ATK. |
| **Crowd Control** | Instant | Renders nearby enemies **Airborne** upon initial strike. |

## The Power of the Wraith Passive

The most complex part of the **mongil stardive yeonhwa** kit is her passive, **Wraith**. This skill provides a significant boost to her late-game scaling by linking her elemental damage to her critical hits.

1.  **Crit DMG Conversion:** Her Crit Damage increases based on a portion of her Lightning DMG Boost. This makes Lightning DMG gear mandatory for high-end builds.
2.  **Shadow Teleportation:** By holding the dodge input, Yeonhwa can instantly move to the location of her active Shadow. This allows for incredible bait-and-switch tactics in PvP and boss encounters.
3.  **Shadow Limitation:** Only one Shadow can exist at a time. If you generate a new one, the previous one disappears.

## Optimized Combat Rotation

To get the most out of **mongil stardive yeonhwa**, follow this standard combat rotation to maximize Shadow uptime and Lightning Affliction:

1.  **Initiate with Thunder Chain Slash:** Close the distance to the target and apply initial pressure.
2.  **Complete Basic Strings:** Cycle through the "Bloody Bamboo" string to ensure a Shadow is active on the field.
3.  **Trigger Ultimate (Storm Call):** Use this when the enemy is grouped to apply Airborne status and enter the Lightning Strike state.
4.  **Spam Infused Basics:** While in the Lightning Strike state, your Shadow deals bonus damage and every hit contributes to Lightning Affliction.
5.  **Tactical Evasion:** Use the Wraith passive teleport (hold dodge) to reposition if the boss prepares a ground-targeted attack.

> **💡 Tip:** If a teammate lands a critical hit while Yeonhwa is in the reserve slot, her "Seized Opportunity" switch skill can be triggered, allowing her to enter the field with a sweeping slash that inflicts Lightning Affliction immediately.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best element to pair with mongil stardive yeonhwa?**

A: Since Yeonhwa focuses on Lightning DMG and Affliction, pairing her with characters who can shred Lightning resistance or provide "Conductive" debuffs is ideal. Characters like Cloud can provide the necessary frontline pressure while Yeonhwa focuses on backstab damage.

**Q: Does the Shadow copy all of Yeonhwa's skills?**

A: No, the Shadow generated by "Bloody Bamboo" primarily copies her Basic Attack actions. However, while under the effects of "Storm Call," the Shadow's contribution increases significantly as it deals bonus damage equal to a portion of her total attack power.

**Q: How do I teleport to my Shadow?**

A: You must have the **Wraith** passive active. While a Shadow is on the field, simply hold the dodge/sprint button instead of tapping it. Yeonhwa will instantly swap places with her Shadow, which is excellent for dodging area-of-effect (AoE) attacks.

**Q: Is Yeonhwa viable for F2P players?**

A: Yes, as a 4-star unit, she is much easier to obtain and "max out" compared to 5-star alternatives. Her high scaling with Lightning DMG makes her one of the most cost-effective DPS units in the 2026 version of the game.
