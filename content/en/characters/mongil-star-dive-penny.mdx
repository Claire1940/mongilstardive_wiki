## Mastering the Mongil Star Dive Penny Playstyle

The core of Penny's gameplay revolves around the **Electric Charge** passive. Unlike other characters who simply wait for cooldowns, Penny must actively generate and spend "Coins."

### The Coin Mechanic Explained
1.  **Generation:** Landing the 3rd hit of her Basic Attack sequence generates one Coin. You can also gain a Coin by performing a successful Critical Dodge.
2.  **Consumption:** Spending a Coin enhances her **Electric Overload** (Special Skill). Instead of a simple leap-back shot, she fires an overloaded arrow that triggers a massive Lightning Explosion and subsequent Lightning Bolts.
3.  **Charge Attacks:** While holding a Coin, Penny can perform a Charge Attack. This restores 20 Stamina and grants a massive 50% DMG boost to her next Special Skill used within 4 seconds.

### Scaling Crit via Lightning DMG
Penny has a unique stat conversion: her **Crit Rate increases by 30% of her Lightning DMG Boost**. This means you should prioritize Lightning DMG on your gear over raw Crit Rate substats, as you essentially get two stats for the price of one. However, note that this bonus Crit Rate is capped at 30%.

## Skill Breakdown and Multipliers

Penny’s damage is heavily weighted toward her enhanced Special Skill and her Ultimate. Understanding the multipliers helps in timing your "Jackpot" windows.

### Titanium Bow (Basic Attack)
*   **1st/2nd Hit:** 15.6% / 29.4% DMG.
*   **3rd Hit (Coin Gen):** 54.1% DMG + 54.1% Electric DMG.
*   **Charge Attack:** 63.7% Pierce + 63.7% Explosion DMG.

### Electric Overload (Special Skill)
This is Penny's bread and butter. Without a Coin, it’s a simple 19.9% DMG arrow. With a Coin, it transforms into a multi-stage nuke:
*   **Electric Arrow:** 270.4% DMG.
*   **Overloaded Arrow:** 270.4% DMG.
*   **Lightning Explosion:** 270.4% DMG.

### Thunderstorm Shot (Ultimate)
Penny fires a massive electric arrow (466.5% DMG) that inflicts **Lightning Affliction**. This debuff reduces the target's Lightning RES by 15% for 5 seconds, creating the perfect window for a Coin-enhanced Special Skill follow-up.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not use your Ultimate while your Special Skill is on cooldown. You want the 15% RES shred to apply specifically to your Overloaded Arrow hits.

## Is Penny Worth Your 5-Star Selector?

When choosing your 5-star from the selector, you must weigh Penny against the current meta. 

*   **Choose Penny if:** You enjoy a "hit-and-run" playstyle, you already have Lightning-support artifacts, or you plan on investing in her Awakenings. She is arguably the most fun character to play due to the active Coin management.

Penny is a "reward for investment" character. She holds her own in World content and Conquest (T1 ranking), but she only reaches her "S-Tier" ceiling once you master the rhythm of her Basic Attack's 3rd hit and the subsequent Charge Attack buff.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I generate Coins faster on Penny?**

A: The fastest way to generate Coins is to complete your 3-hit Basic Attack combo. However, in boss fights, it is often safer to fish for a "Critical Dodge" (dodging right as an attack hits), which grants a Coin instantly without needing to stand still for a combo.

**Q: Does Lightning DMG Boost help Penny if I already have 100% Crit Rate?**

A: No. Penny's passive conversion from Lightning DMG Boost to Crit Rate is capped at 30%. If your equipment already puts you at the Crit cap, the passive conversion provides no additional benefit. In that scenario, focus on Crit DMG or ATK% substats instead.

**Q: Is Penny better than Flare for boss fights?**

A: Generally, no. Flare is a Destroyer, meaning she breaks boss shields much faster. Penny is a Fighter/Ranger, making her better for sustained, safe damage from a distance and clearing waves in Dimensional Rifts.

**Q: What is the "Jackpot" mechanic mentioned in her A6?**

A: At Awakening 6, Penny has a 40% chance to trigger a Jackpot when generating a Coin. This lasts for 5 seconds, giving her a 24% Lightning DMG boost and a massive 60% cooldown reduction on her Special Skill, allowing her to spam overloaded arrows for the duration.
