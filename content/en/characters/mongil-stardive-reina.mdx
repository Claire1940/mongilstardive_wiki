## The Lore of the Colossal Farmer’s Daughter

Reina’s journey is one of the most grounded yet extraordinary tales in the world of Manafall. Born into a remote village on the outskirts of the kingdom, she was the daughter of two "ordinary" farmers who possessed strength far beyond the average human. Legend has it that on the night of her birth, both parents dreamt of holding a bear, a symbolic omen of the raw, untamed power Reina would eventually inherit.

By the age of five, Reina was already stronger than the oxen her family would have typically used to plow the fields. Her family’s decision not to own livestock wasn't just a matter of poverty; it was a matter of efficiency. Why maintain an expensive ox when a young girl could pull the plow faster and more effectively? This upbringing forged her into a kindhearted but physically overwhelming individual.

Her path to knighthood was equally unconventional. While she struggled with the academic rigors of the knightly entrance exams, failing the written portion by a narrow margin, her performance in the physical trials was so terrifying that the examiners felt they had no choice but to admit her. They feared that if someone with Reina's power were rejected, she might eventually turn to a life of banditry, posing a catastrophic threat to the kingdom’s stability.

### Active Skills and Passives

Reina’s kit is designed for single-target pressure. Her passive, **Superconductive Body**, is the cornerstone of her build path, as it increases her Neutralization DMG based on her Maximum HP. This creates a unique gearing challenge where players must balance raw Attack stats with survivability to ensure she can effectively "Groggy" bosses.

*   **Spear of Retribution (Normal ATK):** A series of spear thrusts that transition into blunt weapon strikes. The 4th hit is crucial as it generates Lightning Energy.
*   **Variant Attack:** By holding the attack button with at least 2 charges of Lightning Energy, Reina enters a defensive stance, taking reduced damage while preparing a heavy strike. If she is hit during this window, she gains **Steel Retaliation**, which buffs her Lightning DMG and provides HP recovery.
*   **Form 1 - Thunderstorm (Active):** A rapid-fire sequence of five thrusts followed by a heavy jab. Landing the final hit is essential for energy sustain.
*   **Secret Technique - Radiant Dragon (Ultimate):** Reina leaps into the air and hurls her spear, dealing massive Lightning DMG and inflicting **Lightning Affliction** on all enemies hit.

> **💡 Tip:** Always aim to trigger the 3rd Hit of your Variant Attack. Not only does it deal the highest damage, but the HP restoration from Steel Retaliation is vital for keeping Reina healthy during long Conquest fights.

## Best Build Strategies for Reina

To get the most out of your **mongil stardive reina** build, you must lean into her HP-scaling passive. While most Destroyers prioritize pure Attack and Critical Rate, Reina benefits significantly from a "Bruiser" setup.

1.  **Prioritize HP Substats:** Since her Neutralization DMG scales with Max HP, increasing her health pool directly improves her utility in boss fights.
2.  **Lightning DMG Bonus:** As all her skills deal elemental damage, a Lightning DMG goblet or accessory is mandatory for maintaining competitive DPS.
3.  **Energy Regeneration:** Reina's ramp-up time is her biggest drawback. Equipping gear that boosts energy gain or reduces skill cooldowns can help her reach her 3rd-tier Variant Attacks more frequently.

> **Warning:** Avoid building Reina as a glass cannon. Without a significant HP pool, she loses her primary identity as a Neutralization specialist and becomes a subpar version of other Lightning DPS units.

## FAQ

**Q: Is mongil stardive reina good for F2P players?**

A: Yes. As a 4-star (SR) unit, she is much easier to obtain and awaken than 5-star alternatives. While she requires specific gear to shine, she is a very solid investment for players who need a reliable Lightning Destroyer for boss content.

**Q: Why does my Reina keep missing her attacks in the open world?**

A: Reina’s attack animations are very linear and highly dependent on the camera angle. To fix this, try to lock onto targets whenever possible and avoid using her Active 01 (Thunderstorm) unless the enemy is stationary or stunned.

**Q: Which skill should I prioritize leveling first?**

A: Focus on her **Normal ATK (Spear of Retribution)** first, as the majority of her utility comes from the Variant Attack and the Steel Retaliation buff. After that, prioritize her Ultimate for the Lightning Affliction debuff.

**Q: Does Reina need her signature weapon to be viable in 2026?**

A: While her signature weapon provides a significant boost to her Lightning Energy generation, she can perform well with any high-HP or Lightning DMG spear. However, for T1 Conquest performance, the signature weapon is highly recommended.
