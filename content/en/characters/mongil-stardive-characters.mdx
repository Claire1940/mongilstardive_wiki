## Team Composition and Synergy Strategies

Creating an effective squad involves more than just picking the highest-rated **mongil stardive characters**. You must consider how their abilities interact. In the current 2026 meta, "Tag-Team" synergies are vital.

1.  **Elemental Resonance:** Having three characters of the same element grants a "Monochrome" buff, significantly boosting that element's damage. This is ideal for speed-running specific bosses.
2.  **The Balanced Trio:** A standard team usually consists of one Tank (Earth), one DPS (Fire/Lightning), and one Support (Ice/Wind). This setup is the safest for progressing through the main story.
3.  **Status Effect Chaining:** Some characters, like Ophelia, can freeze enemies, which allows Fire characters like Flare to trigger a "Melt" reaction for massive critical damage.
