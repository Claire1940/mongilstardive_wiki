## Detailed Skill Breakdown

The core of Gabi's gameplay revolves around her ability to inflict **Petrification** and manage her **Spiritual Power**. This resource allows her to unleash "Crushing Blows" that deal significantly higher damage and offer defensive utility.

### Basic Attack: Dokkaebi Bat
Gabi swings her bat wildly to deal Physical DMG. Her 4th hit generates a shockwave that deals Earth DMG. 
*   **Crushing Blow:** By holding the attack button at 3 stacks of Spiritual Power, Gabi triggers a Crushing Blow. This move inflicts Petrification and Earth DMG. While this is active, Gabi gains a Damage Reduction effect, making her difficult to interrupt.
*   **Spinning Bat Attack:** Triggered by holding the attack button after a Switch Skill, Gabi hurls her bat in a circle for AoE Physical DMG.

### Active Skill: Create Gold!
Gabi leaps toward a target and slams the ground. This erupts rocks around the enemy, dealing Earth DMG in a small radius. It is her primary gap-closer and is vital for maintaining uptime on mobile bosses.

### Ultimate: Dokkaebi's Domain
This is Gabi's signature move and why she is so highly valued in 2026. She summons a **Wooden Guardian Statue** that slams the ground for massive Earth DMG.
*   **Buffs:** The statue enhances Gabi's basic attacks, grants her Spiritual Power periodically, and provides Earth DMG and DEF boosts to the entire team.
*   **Affliction:** It inflicts both Petrification and Earth Affliction, making it a premier CC tool for crowd control.

### Passive: Dokkaebi Power
Gabi gains 1 stack of Spiritual Power on her 4th basic attack hit. Additionally, she has a chance to generate a **Rock** on the battlefield.
*   **Rock Mechanic:** These rocks can be destroyed by Physical or Earth DMG. When destroyed, they deal Physical DMG and Petrification to nearby enemies while granting Gabi another stack of Spiritual Power.

> **💡 Tip:** To ramp up Gabi's damage quickly, focus on hitting the 4th attack of her basic combo as often as possible to spawn Rocks, then use her AoE skills to shatter them for instant stacks.

## Best Team Synergy for Gabi

Since Gabi is an Earth-element Fighter, she thrives in teams that can capitalize on Earth DMG buffs and Petrification.

1.  **Earth Resonance:** Pair her with other Earth units to benefit from the DEF and DMG boosts provided by her Ultimate.
2.  **CC Chainers:** Characters that can extend the duration of Petrification or benefit from immobilized enemies work best.
3.  **Physical/Earth Destroyers:** Since her Rocks need to be destroyed to grant stacks, having teammates with wide-reaching Physical or Earth AoE can help Gabi ramp up faster.

## How to Play Gabi Effectively

To master **mongil star dive gabi**, you must learn the rhythm of her "Crushing Blow." You should not just spam basic attacks. Instead, watch your Spiritual Power gauge. Once you hit 3 stacks, look for an opening where the enemy is about to use a Special Attack. 

Using **Crushing Blow** during an enemy's special attack can momentarily neutralize them. If you successfully neutralize an enemy while you have the "Slugger's Will" buff (gained from her Switch Skill "Hoorah!"), you immediately consume the buff to regain all 3 stacks of Spiritual Power, allowing for back-to-back heavy hits.

> **Warning:** Gabi is vulnerable during her ramp-up phase. If you are forced to dodge frequently, you may lose your stack-building momentum. Use her Switch Skill to enter the field with "Slugger's Will" already active to mitigate this.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Gabi better than Sangun in Mongil: Star Dive?**

A: It depends on the content. Sangun generally has faster AoE clearing for timed Rifts, but **mongil star dive gabi** is superior for boss encounters (Conquest) due to her Ultimate's massive damage and team-wide buffs.

**Q: How do I gain Spiritual Power stacks quickly?**

A: Land the 4th hit of your basic attack combo to gain a stack and potentially spawn a Rock. Destroying these Rocks with Earth or Physical damage provides an additional stack. Also, using her Switch Skill "Hoorah!" provides "Slugger's Will," which can instantly refill stacks if you neutralize an enemy.

**Q: What does the Petrification status effect do?**

A: Petrification immobilizes enemies for a short duration. Furthermore, Gabi's Crushing Blow deals increased damage to enemies that are already Petrified, creating a powerful damage loop.

**Q: Can Gabi be used as a solo tank?**

A: While Gabi has high HP and DEF, she is primarily a Fighter (DPS). She can off-tank thanks to the Damage Reduction on her Crushing Blow and the DEF boost from her Ultimate, but in 2026's hardest content, she still benefits from having a dedicated support or protector.
