## Skills and Combat Mechanics

The core of the **mongil star dive yeonhwa** gameplay revolves around the "Shadow" mechanic. Unlike other characters who rely purely on raw stats, Yeonhwa's efficiency is determined by how many shadows she can maintain and how she uses them to reposition during intense boss fights.

### Active Skills

1.  **Bloody Bamboo (Basic Attack):** Yeonhwa delivers a series of quick slashes with her bamboo blade, dealing Physical DMG. The final hit of this combo generates a **[Shadow]** that follows her actions.
2.  **Thunder Chain Slash (Special Skill):** A gap-closer that allows Yeonhwa to dash toward an enemy and deliver multiple slashes. This skill can be recast within a short window, making it excellent for dodging telegraphed enemy attacks while maintaining offensive pressure.
3.  **Storm Call (Ultimate Skill):** Yeonhwa uses her blade as a lightning rod, striking the area with a massive bolt. This renders enemies **Airborne** and triggers the **[Lightning Strike]** state. During this state, all her attacks are converted to Lightning DMG and apply the Lightning Affliction debuff.
4.  **Seized Opportunity (Switch Skill):** When swapping Yeonhwa into the field, she performs a sweeping slash. There is a random chance to generate a shadow, though players should note this shadow occasionally "does nothing" unless triggered by other abilities.

### Passive Ability: Wraith

Yeonhwa's passive, **Wraith**, is what defines her as a late-game powerhouse. It converts a portion of her Lightning DMG Boost into Crit Damage. Furthermore, it allows her to teleport. By holding the dodge input, Yeonhwa can instantly move to the location of an active Shadow, providing unparalleled mobility in the **Mongil: Star Dive** meta.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the mongil star dive yeonhwa character good for beginners?**

A: Yes, she is an excellent starter DPS. While her shadow mechanics have a learning curve, her raw damage output and ability to dodge using her Special Skill make her very forgiving in early-game content.

**Q: Why does my Shadow sometimes do nothing?**

A: This is a built-in mechanic of her Switch Skill, "Seized Opportunity." There is a set chance the generated shadow will be a "dummy" clone. However, shadows generated by her Basic Attack and Ultimate Skill will always mimic her actions.

**Q: What is the best stat to focus on for artifacts?**

A: You should prioritize **Lightning DMG Boost** and **Crit Rate**. Because her passive "Wraith" converts Lightning DMG into Crit DMG, you get double the value from Lightning-specific gear.

**Q: How do I teleport to my Shadow?**

A: While a Shadow is active on the field, simply hold down the dodge/dash button. Yeonhwa will instantly swap places with the Shadow, which is perfect for escaping boss ultimates or repositioning behind a shielded enemy.
