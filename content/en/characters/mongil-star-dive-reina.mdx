Reina's kit is uniquely built around her HP pool, as her passive ability directly links her defensive stats to her offensive utility.

## Skill Mechanics and Lightning Energy

The core of playing **mongil star dive reina** effectively lies in the management of "Lightning Energy." Unlike some characters who simply spam skills on cooldown, Reina requires a rhythmic approach to combat. You must build energy through her normal attack chain and specific skill finishers to unleash her devastating Variant Attacks.

### Spear of Retribution (Normal Attack)
Reina’s basic combo involves a series of spear thrusts that transition into a blunt weapon strike. The 4th hit of this chain is critical, as it charges her Lightning Energy. 
*   **Variant Attack:** By holding the attack button, Reina consumes 2, 4, or 6 energy charges to perform enhanced hits.
*   **Steel Retaliation:** During a Variant Attack, Reina gains damage reduction. If she is hit during this window, she gains "Steel Retaliation," which boosts her Lightning DMG and provides a small HP heal on the final hit.

### Active Skills

| Skill Name | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Thunderstorm** | Active | A 5-hit rapid thrust. The final jab charges Lightning Energy on hit. |

| **Celestial Lightning Spear** | Tag/Switch | A dash-in attack that detonates energy and applies afflictions when swapping. |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Always prioritize landing the final hit of "Thunderstorm." Missing the finisher prevents the energy charge, which significantly delays your ability to use Variant Attacks and slows down your overall DPS rotation.

## Game Mode Performance and Tier List

In the current 2026 meta, Reina is a "niche specialist." She is not an all-rounder like some 5-star units, but she possesses high value in specific environments.

### Conquest (Tier 1)
This is where Reina truly belongs. In single-target boss fights, her heavy animations and narrow hitboxes are less of a liability. Her **Neutralization DMG** is top-tier for a 4-star unit, allowing her to stun bosses frequently. Because Conquest bosses often have predictable patterns, her "Steel Retaliation" mechanic is easier to trigger, turning enemy aggression into a damage buff for her.

### World / Path (Tier 2)
In the open world, Reina can feel frustrating. Her heavy attack animations and camera-dependent sweeps often result in missed strikes against mobile mobs. The time required to ramp up Lightning Energy can make small skirmishes feel longer than they need to be. Without high Awakening levels, she is generally outclassed by faster Lightning units.

### Dimensional Rift (Tier 3)
Reina struggles significantly in the Rift. The timed nature of this content demands high AoE and fast ramp-up speeds, neither of which are Reina's strengths. Furthermore, because her Neutralization DMG scales with HP, players often face a difficult choice: build for HP to break shields or build for ATK to beat the clock. In 2026, most players prefer pure DPS units for Rift farming.

## Advanced Combat Tactics

Mastering the "Steel Retaliation" window is the mark of a great Reina player. Because this buff is granted when Reina takes a hit during the preparation of a Variant Attack, you should intentionally time your heavy attacks to coincide with enemy strikes. This "counter-tanking" style allows you to maintain offensive pressure while sustaining through damage.

*   **The Rotation:** Start with a Tag-in (Celestial Lightning Spear) to apply Lightning Affliction. Follow up with the full Normal Attack chain to build 2-4 charges. Use Thunderstorm to reach 6 charges, then hold for a 3rd Hit Variant Attack to trigger the HP restore and massive Lightning DMG.
*   **Positioning:** Because her sweeps are camera-dependent, try to keep enemies directly in front of you. Avoid getting surrounded, as her lateral coverage is poor.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Reina worth investing in as a F2P player?**

A: Yes, especially if you lack a strong Lightning-element shield breaker. While she requires more skill to play than some other units, her performance in Conquest makes her a valuable asset for boss progression.

**Q: Which skill should I prioritize for upgrades?**

A: Focus on her Normal Attack (Spear of Retribution) first, as it dictates your energy generation and Variant Attack damage. Her Ultimate, Radiant Dragon, should be the second priority for the Lightning Affliction uptime.

**Q: Does the mongil star dive reina build require specific gear?**

A: While she can function with general DPS gear, she truly excels with HP-heavy sets. Because her Neutralization DMG scales with HP, look for gear that offers a balance of survivability and elemental damage.

**Q: How do I fix her "missed" attacks in the open world?**

A: This is a common issue due to her narrow hitboxes. To mitigate this, try to use her Tag-out skill to group enemies or use an ally with crowd control (CC) before switching Reina in to perform her heavy thrusts.
