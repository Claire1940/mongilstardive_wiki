## Performance Optimization Tips

If your device barely meets the **mongil stardive android requirements**, follow these steps to improve your in-game performance:

*   **Enable Game Mode:** Most modern Android phones (Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus) have a built-in "Game Turbo" or "Game Booster" mode. Ensure this is active to prioritize CPU/GPU resources for the game.
*   **Adjust Frame Rate:** If the game feels "choppy," lock the frame rate to 30 FPS in the settings. A consistent 30 FPS is often better than a fluctuating 60 FPS.
*   **Clear Cache:** Periodically clear the app cache through your Android settings if you notice the game slowing down after several hours of play.
*   **Disable Background Apps:** Close resource-heavy apps like Discord, Chrome, or YouTube before launching the game to free up as much of your 8GB/12GB RAM as possible.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are playing on a tablet, the game may default to a higher resolution. Manually lowering the resolution scale to 80% can significantly boost FPS without a massive loss in visual clarity.
