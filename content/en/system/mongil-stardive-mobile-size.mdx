## FAQ

**Q: Can I play Mongil: Star Dive on an SD card?**

A: While Android allows moving some data to an SD card, it is not recommended for this game. The slow read/write speeds of most SD cards will cause significant stuttering and long loading screens. For the best experience, keep the game on your device's internal UFS storage.

**Q: Why is the mongil stardive mobile size different on my friend's phone?**

A: The total size can vary based on the resolution of the assets downloaded (Standard vs. HD), the number of language packs installed, and the amount of cached data accumulated during gameplay.

**Q: Is there a "Lite" version of the game?**

A: Currently, Netmarble does not offer a dedicated Lite version. However, you can manually create a "lite" experience by setting all graphics to "Low" and disabling high-quality audio in the settings menu, which prevents the game from downloading additional elective assets.

**Q: How often does the mongil stardive mobile size increase?**

A: Typically, the game receives a major content update every 4-6 weeks. These updates usually add between 500MB and 1.5GB to the total storage requirement. It is wise to check your available space before every major patch.
