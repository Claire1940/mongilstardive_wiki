## Understanding the Monsterling System

A core mechanic that sets this game apart on all **mongil star dive platforms** is the Monsterling system. In the lore of Belana, a monster is not truly deceased as long as its core—the Monsterling—remains intact. This allows for a unique "link" between the tamer and the creature.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when handling Monsterlings in the game world. While they are dormant in this state, pouring hot water on a Monsterling will cause it to instantly revert to its original (and potentially hostile) form!

Tamers can bind monsters to their command, allowing these creatures to transform into their Monsterling forms for easy transport. During combat, you can call upon these bound monsters to perform devastating "linked" attacks, which are essential for clearing high-level dungeons in the Deadlands.
