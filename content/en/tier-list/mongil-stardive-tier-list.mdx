## T0: The Meta-Defining Elites

The T0 tier represents the pinnacle of power in 2026. These characters possess kits that are either overloaded with utility or provide unmatched damage numbers that ignore many of the game's standard limitations.

### Esther (Wind)
Esther remains the undisputed queen of AOE damage. Her unique "Dust Cloud" mechanic not only buffs her own attack but also confuses enemies, providing a massive boost to team survivability. While she does face some cooldown hurdles, her auto-attacks deal wind damage in an area while she is inside her field, making her a sustained damage powerhouse.

### Flare (Fire)
If your goal is to take down massive bosses in Conquest mode, Flare is your primary solution. Her "Blaze Zone" creates a persistent area of extra fire damage, and her ultimate is a triple threat: it knocks enemies airborne, applies Fire Affliction, and significantly decreases enemy defense. She is widely considered the best boss killer in the current **mongil stardive tier list**.

### Sangun (Wind)
Sangun is favored by many high-level players for his straightforward yet devastating kit. His "Wild Fervor" mechanic rewards players for successful counter-attacks by boosting the damage of his special and ultimate skills. With the ability to summon a tiger that knocks back enemies, he excels at controlling the battlefield in multi-wave scenarios like the Dimensional Rift.

## T0.5: Superior Sub-DPS and Supports

These characters are often just as vital as T0 units but usually fulfill a more specific role within a team. They are essential for rounding out your roster.

*   **Ophelia (Ice):** A crit-damage specialist. While she lacks the massive AOE of Esther, her single-target burst is top-tier. She can use her special skill twice at the cost of an increased cooldown, allowing for massive "burst windows."
*   **Jiwon (Wind):** The best pure support for Wind teams. She provides a unique "Divine Harmony" buff that simultaneously increases Wind DMG and decreases Stamina consumption. She is the perfect partner for Esther.
*   **Gabi (Earth):** A technical fighter who uses "Spiritual Power" charges. Her ability to petrify enemies increases the damage they take from her crushing blows, making her a formidable force against high-health targets.

## Game Mode Specialization

Not every character on the **mongil stardive tier list** performs equally across all modes. Some excel at wave clearing, while others are built for endurance.

### Story Mode (World / Path)
For the main story, mobility and AOE are king. Characters like **Mina** and **Sangun** are excellent here because they can pull enemies together and delete them instantly. Healers like **Daisy** are generally not recommended for the story, as speed is usually more beneficial than sustain.

### Conquest (Boss Focus)
This mode requires high "Neutralization" damage to break boss shields. **Flare** and **Cloud** excel here. Cloud, in particular, generates ice crystals that can be consumed to deal massive stagger damage, making him a T1 unit that punches well above his weight class in this specific mode.

### Dimensional Rift (Timed Waves)
In the Rift, you are fighting the clock. **Esther** and **Benjamin** are the stars here. Benjamin's ability to summon clones and keep enemies airborne prevents them from attacking while your main DPS cleans up the field.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be cautious when investing heavily in **Leeho** or **Daisy**. Current data suggests they fall off significantly in late-game content compared to 4-star units with high awakening levels.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall character in the mongil stardive tier list?**

A: Currently, **Esther** is widely considered the best overall due to her unmatched AOE damage and utility in both the Dimensional Rift and Story mode. However, **Flare** is the superior choice for boss-specific content.

**Q: Is it worth leveling up 4-star characters?**

A: Yes. Many 4-star characters, when fully awakened, can outperform T1 or even T0.5 5-star units. **Yeonhwa** is a prime example; she starts weak but becomes a lightning-damage monster once her crit-scaling passives are unlocked through awakening.

**Q: Why is Francis ranked differently on various sites?**

A: Francis is a polarizing unit. She provides excellent healing and Earth buffs, but her effectiveness is heavily tied to her specific Character Artifact. Without her artifact, she drops to T2, but with it, she can be an SS-tier support for Earth teams.

**Q: How often does the meta change?**

A: As a new title, balance patches are frequent. This **mongil stardive tier list** is accurate as of April 2026, but new character releases like Nari are expected to shift the support meta significantly in the coming months.
