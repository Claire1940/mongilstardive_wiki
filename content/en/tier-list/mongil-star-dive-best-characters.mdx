## Deep Dive: The SS-Tier Powerhouses

Identifying the **mongil star dive best characters** involves looking at how their skills interact with team-wide buffs. The SS-tier units are considered "must-haves" because they provide unique mechanics that cannot be easily replaced by lower-rarity alternatives.

### Esther (Wind DPS)
Esther is widely regarded as the premier AOE damage dealer at launch. Her ability to clear waves of enemies makes her indispensable for the Dimensional Rift and story progression. Her damage output is significantly enhanced when paired with other Wind units, allowing her to shred through enemy health bars with minimal effort.

### Jiwon (Wind Support/CC)
Jiwon is the backbone of the "Golden Start" strategy. Her **Divine Harmony** buff is a game-changer, as it simultaneously increases Wind DMG for the entire team while decreasing Stamina consumption. Furthermore, her special skill provides essential crowd control by grouping enemies and knocking them airborne, setting up perfect windows for your main DPS to strike.

### Francis (Earth Healer)
Survival is just as important as damage in *Mongil: Star Dive*. Francis offers a "safety net" with her automatic healing mechanic. If your active character's HP falls below a certain threshold, Francis triggers a heal to keep the fight going. Her Tag-Out skill also provides an ATK buff, ensuring that she contributes to the team's offensive pressure even while performing support duties.
