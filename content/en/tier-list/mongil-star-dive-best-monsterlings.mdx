## Reroll Guide and Resource Management

Rerolling for the **mongil star dive best monsterlings** is notably more difficult in this title compared to other Netmarble games. The "salting" method (using email aliases like yourname+1@gmail.com) is currently non-functional.

### How to Reroll in 2026
1.  **New Account:** Launch the game and sign in with a fresh email address.
2.  **Tutorial:** Complete the prologue until you unlock the summoning menu (approximately 15-20 minutes).
3.  **Eternal Promise Banner:** Use your first 20 draws here to secure a guaranteed 5-star. Aim for **Flare**.
4.  **Limited Banner:** Use any remaining currency on Esther's banner.
5.  **Reset:** If you fail to get Flare or Esther, you must log out and use a completely different email address.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Resource fatigue is a significant factor in *Mongil: Star Dive*. Avoid spreading your upgrade materials across too many units. Focus on your core team of three first.
