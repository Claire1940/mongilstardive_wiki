## Understanding Character Classes and Roles

Before building your squad, you must understand the three core pillars of combat in Mongil: Star Dive. Each team usually consists of three active members, and their class determines their primary function on the battlefield.

| Class | Primary Function | Combat Specialty |
|---|---|---|
| **Fighter** | Multi-target DPS | Gains power when surrounded; ideal for clearing waves of smaller enemies. |
| **Assassin** | Single-target Burst | Deals massive damage to Staggered enemies; highest critical potential. |

## Advanced Team Building: Synergies and Mechanics

To truly optimize the **mongil stardive best team**, you must look beyond just character rarity. The interaction between stats like **Neutralization DMG** and **Elemental Affliction** is what separates average players from the elite.

### 1. Neutralization and Stagger
Every boss has a Stagger Gauge. When this is depleted, the boss enters a "Break" state, taking increased damage. Assassins like Esther or Angel deal significantly more damage during this window. Always lead with your Destroyer (Flare or Ellie) to initiate the break, then swap to your Assassin for the kill.

### 2. Elemental Affliction
When you hit an enemy with the same element repeatedly, you apply an Affliction. This reduces their specific elemental resistance. A Mono-Element team (e.g., three Earth units) can keep this debuff active 100% of the time, leading to the highest theoretical damage output in the game.

### 3. The Role of Support Units
Characters like **Francis** and **Bonnie** are versatile additions to any **mongil stardive best team**. Francis offers universal attack buffs and healing, making her a "safe" pick for any elemental composition if you are missing a specific 5-star unit.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best team for the Conquest mode?**

A: The best team for Conquest usually includes **Flare**, **Esther**, and **Jiwon**. Because Conquest has a strict 5-minute timer, you need Flare's Destroyer capabilities to break the boss quickly and Esther's burst damage to finish the fight before time runs out.

**Q: Can I use a team with three different elements?**

A: Yes, this is known as a "3-Element Team." While you lose out on the massive resistance shred of a Mono-Element team, you gain versatility. This is often the **mongil stardive best team** for exploring the main story where enemy weaknesses change frequently between waves.
