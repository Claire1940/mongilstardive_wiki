## Fire Element Optimization: Flare and Angel

Fire teams in Mongil: Star Dive excel at dealing Damage Over Time (DOT) through the Fire Affliction debuff. Flare is the undisputed queen of this element, but 4-star units like Angel can provide incredible value when built correctly.

### Flare’s Boss-Melting Setup
Flare’s **mongil stardive best build** focuses on her Ultimate, **Cataclysm**, which shreds enemy defense. You should prioritize equipment that boosts Fire Elemental Damage and reduces cooldowns. 

1. **Blaze Zone Management**: Ensure Flare stays within her Blaze Zone to maintain the Fire DMG buff.
2. **Defense Shred**: Use her Ultimate specifically when a boss is in a Staggered state to multiply the damage.
3. **Team Synergy**: Pair her with Leeho or Angel to maintain 100% uptime on Fire Affliction.

### Angel: Ranged Assassin Build
Angel requires a high-risk, high-reward playstyle. Despite being ranged, she deals 20% more damage when "Locked and Loaded" (standing near the enemy).

*   **Best Equipment**: **Hahnul's Roar Set**. The 2-set bonus increases Fire DMG after an Ultimate, while the 4-set bonus boosts Crit Damage against enemies with Fire Affliction.
*   **Awakening Priority**: Aim for **A2 (Unstoppable Pursuit)**, which doubles her Locked and Loaded bonus to 40% and applies it to all skills, not just basic attacks.

## Artifacts and Monsterling Synergies

Artifacts are the most complex part of any **mongil stardive best build**. They offer "Fusion Effects" that trigger only when specific conditions are met, such as landing a critical hit or performing a successful dodge.

### Essential Artifacts for 2026
*   **Held Close by Nature**: Essential for any Wind Fighter (Sangun, Esther). It scales with Fighter Proficiency, providing both ATK and Wind DMG boosts.
*   **Overflowing Love**: The go-to artifact for Francis. It converts her healing skills into powerful Critical Damage buffs for the entire team.
*   **Tigerquake Mechanics**: This effect triggers upon counterattacking. If you are not proficient at timing your dodges/counters, this artifact loses 60% of its value. Practice in the training room to master the timing.

### Monsterling Link Chains
Monsterlings provide passive stat auras. For a balanced **mongil stardive best build**, you should generally run one elemental shredder and two DPS boosters.

*   **Avardan & Amon**: These are universal favorites for increasing team-wide DPS.
*   **Mountaintaur**: Specifically used for Conquest mode. It increases Critical Damage against bosses in a Staggered state.
*   **El Dorado Guardian**: A niche but powerful choice for elemental weakness builds, boosting team ATK when you hit the correct element.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the single most important stat for a mongil stardive best build?**

A: While it depends on the character, **Critical Rate** is generally the most important stat in 2026. Many characters, like Yeonhwa and Sangun, have passive abilities or artifacts that only trigger upon landing critical hits.

**Q: Can I use 4-star artifacts in the endgame?**

A: Yes, but only if they are fully fused. A max-fusion 4-star artifact is often better than a base-level 5-star artifact because the Fusion Effects (like Tigerquake) provide massive percentage-based multipliers that base stats cannot match.

**Q: How do I improve my "Neutralization" damage?**

A: Neutralization damage is key for staggering bosses. Characters like Cloud and Ellie specialize in this. To boost it, look for Monsterlings with "Stagger Boost" effects and equip the Mountaintaur link chain for increased damage once the boss is neutralized.

**Q: Is healing necessary for story mode?**

A: Generally, no. Most players find that high-DPS builds with good dodging are enough for the story. However, for Conquest and Dimensional Rifts, bringing a healer like Francis or a support like Ellie (who generates healing snacks) is highly recommended to maintain survivability during long encounters.
