## Understanding Mongil: Star Dive Summoning Rates

The summoning system in *Mongil: Star Dive* is built around a transparent but challenging rate structure. For the primary **mongil star dive banners** in 2026, the base rate for a five-star (SSR) character typically sits at **1%**. While this may seem low compared to some contemporary titles, the game balances this with a pity system and guaranteed pulls after a set number of attempts.

When you pull on a featured banner, such as the Esther or G1 banners, you are not just hunting for a single copy. Character "Advancements" are critical for unlocking a unit's full potential, meaning multiple pulls of the same character are often required to stay competitive in high-level PvE and PvP content.

## The Role of 4-Star Units: Daisy, Raina, and Angel

While 5-star units are the main attraction of **mongil star dive banners**, your 4-star pulls (SR) act as the backbone of your account. In 2026, several 4-star characters have seen "power-crept" stats that allow them to rival basic 5-star units when fully advanced.

1. **Daisy (The Elf):** A very common pull in the current rotation. While some players find her frequency frustrating, a fully advanced Daisy (A5 or higher) provides incredible early-game value due to her low cost and consistent damage output.
2. **Raina:** Often appearing alongside featured banners, Raina is a versatile filler unit that can bridge the gap in teams missing a dedicated healer.
3. **Angel:** Widely considered one of the strongest 4-star units in the game, Angel's kit provides crowd control that is essential for progressing through the mid-game tower challenges.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not overlook 4-star artifacts. While characters are generally more exciting, high-tier artifacts provide the percentage-based stat boosts necessary to clear endgame "Hard Mode" content.

## Summoning Strategy for 2026

To make the most of the **mongil star dive banners**, you should adopt a disciplined approach to your Starlight Crystals. Randomly pulling on every new banner is a quick way to deplete resources without achieving a cohesive team.

### Focus on Advancement
In *Mongil: Star Dive*, a single copy of a 5-star unit is often weaker than a fully maxed-out 4-star unit. If you decide to pull for a character like Esther, aim for at least one or two "Advancements" (A1 or A2). This usually unlocks a passive skill or a significant stat multiplier that makes the character viable for competitive play.

### The "Bait Banner" Phenomenon
Developers often release visually appealing or moderately strong characters right before a major "Meta" shift. When evaluating **mongil star dive banners**, consider the timing. If a major game update is scheduled for the following month, the current banner might be designed to drain your Starlight Crystals. However, if a character like Esther fits your specific elemental needs, pulling is rarely a total loss.

### Pity and Guaranteed Pulls
Most banners in 2026 feature a "Guaranteed 5-star" after a specific number of multis (usually 80-100 summons). If you are within 20 pulls of your pity, it is almost always worth finishing the cycle to secure the SSR, even if you have already pulled a copy of the featured unit.

For more information on the game's development and future updates, you can visit the [official Netmarble website](https://www.netmarble.com/) for announcements regarding upcoming character releases.

## FAQ

**Q: Are the rates for mongil star dive banners better for multi-summons?**

A: While the 1% SSR rate remains the same per pull, multi-summons (10-pulls) often guarantee at least one 4-star character or artifact, making them more efficient for building your general roster than single pulls.

**Q: Should I pull for Esther if I don't have a wind team?**

A: Esther is a powerhouse, but her true potential is unlocked alongside other Wind units like Sangun. If your current team is primarily Fire or Water, you may want to save your crystals for a banner that matches your current elemental investments.

**Q: What happens if I pull a duplicate of a maxed-out character?**

A: Duplicates of characters you have already fully advanced are typically converted into a special currency that can be spent in the "Memory Shop" for high-tier upgrade materials or rare summon tickets.

**Q: Is there a 50/50 system in Mongil: Star Dive?**

A: Currently, most featured **mongil star dive banners** do not use a 50/50 mechanic. If you trigger a 5-star pull on a featured banner, there is a high probability (often 70% or higher) that it will be the featured unit, with the remainder of the pool being filled by the standard SSR roster.
