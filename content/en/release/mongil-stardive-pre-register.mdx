## Understanding the World of Belana

The story of Mongil: Star Dive takes place on the planet Belana, a world divided into three distinct continents. Each region offers unique environmental hazards, monster species, and lore-heavy questlines.

### Centralia
This is the heart of the adventure and the home of the Kingdom of Solismenamus. As the starting point for protagonists Cloud and Verna, Centralia serves as the tutorial hub where players learn the basics of the "Monster Tamers" guild.

### The Far East
A land steeped in tradition and mystery, the Far East is where Baek-Ah lies. This continent is heavily influenced by Eastern mythology, featuring characters like Mina, the Kumiho descended from the Divine Maidens.

### The Deadlands
Once a thriving region, the Deadlands were rendered uninhabitable by a cataclysmic event long ago. Legends say that only the most powerful Tamers can survive the journey into this void-touched territory to find "legendary monsters."

## The Monsterling and Taming Mechanics

One of the standout features that sets this game apart is the biological concept of **Monsterlings**. In Belana, monsters aren't just enemies; they are lifeforms powered by "magic stones."

When a monster exhausts its life force, it doesn't truly die as long as its core—the Monsterling—remains intact. Tamers can bind these cores to themselves, creating a "linked" state.

**Key Mechanics of Monsterlings:**
*   **Transformation**: Bound monsters can shift into their Monsterling forms at their owner's command for easy transport.
*   **The "Hot Water" Rule**: Interestingly, pouring hot water onto a Monsterling in its core form will cause it to instantly revert to its original monster form, even without a Tamer's command.
*   **Communication**: While many monsters are feral, some possess the intelligence for complex communication and even form diplomatic ties with demihuman species.

| Feature | Description | Strategic Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Magic Stone** | The biological power source of all monsters. | Targeted during combat to weaken high-HP bosses. |
| **Linked State** | A spiritual bond between Tamer and Monsterling. | Unlocks "Combined Skills" during high-intensity battles. |
| **Monster Taming** | The act of capturing and befriending wild creatures. | Essential for building a balanced 3-member party. |
