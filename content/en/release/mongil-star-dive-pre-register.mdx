## Understanding the World of Belana

Mongil: Star Dive isn't just about the human characters; it's about the relationship between Tamers and "Monsterlings." The lore states that the world was created by "Bigcat," who used the Threads of Fate to weave reality out of a void. This theme of "threads" and "spools" permeates the entire gacha and upgrade system.

### The Three Continents
1.  **Centralia:** The heart of the Kingdom of Solismenamus and the starting point for Cloud and Verna.
2.  **The Far East:** A land of mystery where the Kumiho, such as the character Mina, originate.
3.  **The Deadlands:** A high-level zone rendered uninhabitable by ancient events, likely housing the game's endgame raids.

### Monsterlings and Magic Stones
Every monster in Belana possesses a "magic stone." When a monster is defeated or exhausted, it reverts to its "Monsterling" form—its core magical essence. As a Tamer, you can bind these Monsterlings to your characters, allowing them to transform and provide "linked" abilities during combat. Interestingly, pouring hot water on a Monsterling in the game world can cause it to revert to its original form instantly, a quirky mechanic that adds flavor to the exploration.

**Q: Can I use the same mongil star dive pre register rewards on multiple servers?**

A: Usually, pre-registration rewards are tied to a single claim per account. Once you claim them on a specific server/character, they cannot be moved. Choose your main server carefully.

**Q: How do I bind a Monsterling to my character?**
