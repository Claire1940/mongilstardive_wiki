### Character and Artifact Rates

The gacha is divided into two primary categories: Characters and Artifacts. Each has its own distinct pity threshold and drop rate. For the 1.0 version, the rates have been buffed following player feedback from previous testing phases.

While the hard pity for characters is set at 90 pulls, players have reported a "soft pity" system where the 1% drop rate begins to scale significantly between 60 and 70 pulls. If you have not seen a gold flash by pull 75, your chances of pulling the featured unit increase exponentially with every subsequent attempt.

## FAQ

**Q: Do the pity counts reset when the mongil star dive 1.0 banners expire?**

A: No, the pity count for limited event banners carries over to the next limited banner. If you are at 80/90 pulls when Esther's banner ends, you will only need 10 pulls on the next character banner to guarantee the featured unit.

**Q: Is there a 50/50 system for 5-star characters?**

A: No. In the 1.0 update, the limited character banner guarantees the featured character (like Esther) whenever you pull a 5-star unit. There is no risk of pulling a standard character on the limited event banner.

**Q: How do I get more Compass of Destiny for limited banners?**

A: Compass of Destiny is primarily obtained through exchanging Diamonds (the premium currency), completing certain high-level achievements, or participating in special launch events like the Maid and Clover In event shop.

**Q: Is it worth pulling for artifacts in version 1.0?**

A: For F2P players, it is generally recommended to focus on characters first. However, if you manage to pull Esther early, her signature artifact can provide a massive boost to her performance. The artifact banner has a lower pity of 80 pulls compared to the character banner.
