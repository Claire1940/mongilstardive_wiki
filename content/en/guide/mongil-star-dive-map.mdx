## Solving Oz Puzzles for Permanent Upgrades

One of the most unique aspects of the **mongil star dive map** is the inclusion of Oz Puzzles. These are not merely distractions; they are the primary method for increasing your maximum stamina capacity. When you encounter the "young lady" associated with these puzzles, completing her challenges grants you a special scroll.

These scrolls can be exchanged for permanent stamina extensions. In 2026, efficient stamina management is the bottleneck for most players. By maximizing your map exploration early, you ensure that you can store more "free" stamina and participate in more endgame Conquests and Dimensional Rifts without needing to refresh constantly.

## Character Roles for Map Clearing

While exploring the **mongil star dive map**, the composition of your three-person team determines how quickly you can clear mobs and bosses. Each role serves a specific purpose during world exploration.

1.  **Fighters:** These are your bread-and-butter for general map clearing. Their damage increases based on the number of nearby enemies, making them perfect for the large groups of monsters found in open-world segments.
2.  **Assassins:** Use these when you encounter elite enemies on the map. They deal massive burst damage to enemies afflicted by Crowd Control (CC) effects like Airborne or Staggered.
3.  **Destroyers:** Essential for map bosses. They are designed to deplete the stagger gauge quickly, allowing your Assassins to finish the job.
4.  **Supports:** Never leave home without one. They provide the healing and buffs necessary to survive long exploration sessions without returning to town.

| Role | Best Used For | Key Mechanic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fighter** | Mob Clearing | Multi-target DMG scaling |
| **Assassin** | Elite/Boss Execution | CC-triggered Burst |
| **Destroyer** | Boss Breaking | Stagger Gauge depletion |
| **Support** | Sustainability | Healing & Elemental Buffs |

## Monsterling Collection and the World Map

The **mongil star dive map** is teeming with Monsterlings waiting to be captured. Capture rates and the quality of the Monsterlings you find are directly tied to your **Nyanko level**. To improve your odds of finding high-grade Monsterlings with rare traits, you must capture as many monsters as possible to level up your Nyanko companion.

### Combining and Mutations
Once you've cleared sections of the map and filled your inventory with Monsterlings, you can use the Combining feature. This allows you to:
*   **Same-Species Combine:** Guarantees the same species but randomizes traits and potentially increases rarity.
*   **Cross-Species Combine:** A 50/50 chance of getting either parent type with new traits.
*   **Mutations:** Following specific recipes (unlocked via discovery) can result in rare Mutant Monsterlings.

> **Warning:** Avoid combining different grades of Monsterlings (e.g., a Rare with a Common). There is a significant risk that the result will default to the lower grade, wasting your higher-tier unit.

## Stamina Management and Progression

A common mistake when exploring the **mongil star dive map** is claiming stamina rewards too early. If your stamina exceeds its maximum capacity (overflow), the natural regeneration rate slows down significantly. This results in a net loss of "free" stamina over time.

To stay efficient in 2026:
*   Only claim stamina from mail or friends when you are below your cap.
*   Spend stamina on **Event Stages** as soon as they unlock.
*   Shift focus to **Character EXP (Path)** once you have a stable team.
*   Farm breakthrough materials only when you hit a level cap to avoid wasting resources on unnecessary map nodes.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I find the last Hidden Chest on the mongil star dive map?**

A: Use the map tracker in the UI. If it shows a chest is missing, check behind waterfalls, inside destructible objects, or on high ledges that require jumping puzzles. Some chests only appear after defeating a specific group of nearby enemies.

**Q: Are Oz Puzzles mandatory for finishing the game?**

A: While not strictly required to see the credits, Oz Puzzles are mandatory for competitive play. They provide the only way to permanently increase your stamina cap, which is vital for farming the materials needed for late-game character breakthroughs.

**Q: Can I see Monsterling locations on the map?**

A: While the map doesn't show individual spawns in real-time, certain Monsterling species are native to specific regions of the **mongil star dive map**. Check your Bestiary to see which zone you need to patrol to find the specific species you want to capture or mutate.

**Q: What is the best team for exploring new map areas?**

A: A balanced team consisting of a **Fighter** (for mobs), a **Destroyer** (for bosses), and a **Support** (for healing) is the most reliable composition for blind exploration. If you find yourself struggling with a boss, ensure your Destroyer is the same element as the boss's weakness to break their stagger gauge faster.
