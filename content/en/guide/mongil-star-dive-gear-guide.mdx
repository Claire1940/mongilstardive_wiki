## Equipment Basics: Types and Stats

Every character in Mongil: Star Dive can be equipped with four distinct types of gear: Headgear, Chestpieces, Gloves, and Footwear. Understanding the relationship between the gear type and its primary stat is the first step toward optimization. While most slots have fixed main stats, the Footwear slot is the most critical for late-game builds due to its variable proficiency bonuses.

### Equipment Slots and Main Stats

| Equipment Type | Fixed Main Stat | Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Headgear** | HP (Health Points) | Survival against burst damage |
| **Chestpieces** | Defense | Reducing sustained physical/elemental damage |
| **Gloves** | Attack | Primary source of raw DPS scaling |
| **Footwear** | Role Proficiency | Multiplies effectiveness for specific classes |

In the early game, your priority should be simply filling every slot. Do not worry about set bonuses until you have a full team equipped with at least 3-star gear. Once you begin crafting 4-star (purple) and 5-star (gold) equipment, the focus shifts toward matching the right gear to the right character role.

## Crafting Your Way to Power

Unlike many other gacha RPGs where gear is exclusively found in RNG drops, Mongil: Star Dive emphasizes a robust crafting system. This feature is unlocked early in Episode 1 via the "Do It Yourself!" side quest. Crafting allows you to target specific sets, reducing the frustration of random loot.

### The Crafting Process
1.  **Access the Menu:** Tap "Crafting" from the main interface.
2.  **Select Your Set:** Choose between general gear or specific high-tier sets like the Abyss or Arbiter sets.
3.  **Gather Materials:** You will need a combination of minerals (mined from the open world) and monster materials (obtained from Requests and Conquest).
4.  **Execute Craft:** Spend Gold and materials to generate the item. Note that while the main stat is fixed (except for Footwear), the substats will be randomized.

> **💡 Tip:** Always mine rare minerals like Rubies and Sapphires whenever you see them on the map. They are often tucked away in corners and are the primary bottleneck for crafting 5-star equipment.

## Understanding Set Bonuses

Set bonuses are the backbone of any meta build. Equipping 2 or 4 pieces of the same equipment series activates unique passive effects that can significantly boost your elemental damage or utility. As of 2026, the Prime Star sets are the gold standard for competitive play.

### Featured Equipment Sets

| Set Name | 2-Piece Effect | 4-Piece Effect | Recommended Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Abyss** | Wind DMG +10% for 5s on Special Skill | Wind DMG +10% for 5s on Critical Hit | Wind DPS (e.g., Esther) |
| **Arbiter** | Ice DMG +10% for 5s on Switch Skill | Ice DMG +10% for 5s vs Ice Affliction | Ice DPS / Supports |

To craft these high-tier sets, you will need specialized materials. For instance, the Abyss set requires **Void Fragments**, **Moonstones**, and **Emeralds**. These are best farmed through the Conquest mode, where bosses grow progressively stronger but yield better rewards.

## Optimizing Footwear and Role Proficiency

A common mistake players make is ignoring the "Role Proficiency" stat on Footwear. Unlike Headgear or Gloves, Footwear main stats only provide a benefit if they match the character's specific role.

*   **Fighter Proficiency:** Boosts stats for Fighters who excel in group combat.
*   **Destroyer Proficiency:** Essential for characters like Flare to charge the enemy's stagger gauge faster.
*   **Assassin Proficiency:** Maximizes burst damage for characters who exploit Crowd Control (CC) debuffs.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not discard Footwear just because the role doesn't match your current main DPS. Keep it in your inventory for secondary team members or future pulls. Role-matched 5-star Footwear is rare and should never be used as mere enhancement fodder.

## Substat Optimization Strategy

Once you have crafted your desired set, the next step in this **mongil star dive gear guide** is "rolling" for the perfect substats. Every piece of equipment can have up to four substats. In Mongil: Star Dive, you can see two substats immediately upon crafting. A third is added when the item reaches Level 3.

### Substat Enhancement Milestones
| Level | Effect |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Lvl 1** | 2 Substats visible |
| **Lvl 3** | 1 New Substat added |
| **Lvl 6** | Random existing Substat enhanced |
| **Lvl 9** | Final Substat added |
| **Lvl 12-18** | Further random enhancements |

The most efficient way to optimize is to stop enhancing an item if the first three substats (at Level 3) are poor. Desirable substats generally include **Crit Rate**, **Crit DMG**, and **ATK**. If you roll multiple Resistance stats on a DPS item, it is often better to use that item as experience material for a new craft, as you recover approximately 70% of the invested experience.

## Farming Materials Efficiently

To sustain a high level of crafting, you must manage your daily activities. You are limited to 5 Requests per day. These should be chosen specifically based on the monster materials required for your next gear piece.

1.  **Daily Requests:** Target monsters that drop the specific components for your 4-star sets.
2.  **Conquest Mode:** This is the primary source for 5-star materials. Once you defeat a Stage 10 boss, you unlock the ability to exchange boss tokens directly for their signature equipment or materials.
3.  **Active Friends:** Add active friends to receive daily stamina. More stamina equals more mining runs and more monster material drops.
4.  **Oz Puzzles:** Complete these hidden map objectives to get scrolls that permanently extend your maximum stamina capacity, allowing for longer farming sessions.

## Role-Specific Gear Focus

Your gear strategy should shift based on the character's role in your three-person team.

*   **Fighters:** Focus on a balance of ATK and HP. Since Fighters deal more damage when surrounded by enemies, survival is just as important as offense.
*   **Assassins:** Pure offensive stats. Look for Crit Rate and Crit DMG substats. Assassins shine when the enemy is Airborne or Staggered, so timing your gear procs with these windows is key.
*   **Destroyers:** Prioritize Destroyer Proficiency on Footwear. Their job is to break the boss's stagger gauge as quickly as possible so your Assassin can move in for the kill.
*   **Supports:** Focus on Cooldown Reduction (if available via sets) and HP/Defense to ensure they stay on the field to provide healing and buffs.

## FAQ

**Q: When should I start focusing on set bonuses?**

A: It is recommended to start focusing on set bonuses once you can reliably craft 4-star (purple) equipment. In the early game, raw stats from any available gear are more beneficial than forcing a low-tier set bonus.

**Q: Can I change the substats on an existing piece of gear?**

A: Currently, you cannot reroll individual substats. You must craft a new version of the item or find a new drop to get a different set of substats. This makes the Level 3 "check" vital for saving resources.

**Q: What is the best way to get Gold for crafting?**

A: Gold is best earned through daily missions and clearing the Dimensional Rift. Late-game crafting costs (16,000+ Gold per piece) add up quickly, so avoid over-enhancing low-tier 3-star gear.

**Q: Does elemental resistance matter in this mongil star dive gear guide?**

A: While offensive stats are generally preferred, specific elemental resistances (Fire, Ice, Wind, Lightning) are very useful for late-game Conquest bosses who deal heavy elemental affliction damage. Keep a few pieces with high resistance for niche encounters.
