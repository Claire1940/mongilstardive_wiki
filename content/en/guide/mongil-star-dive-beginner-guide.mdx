This MONGIL STAR DIVE guide focuses on the first seven days of progression. The goal is to secure a stable core team, clear story gates, and avoid resource waste.

## Day 1 priorities

- Finish tutorial and account linkage.
- Use beginner pulls only after checking reroll targets.
- Push campaign until stamina bottlenecks.

## Resource rules

- Save premium currency for account-defining banners.
- Upgrade one core damage dealer first.
- Do not spread enhancement materials across many low-rarity units.

## Daily loop

1. Claim login and event rewards.
2. Spend stamina on highest-value progression stage.
3. Finish daily missions and limited-time objectives.
4. Review shop refreshes for discounted upgrade items.
