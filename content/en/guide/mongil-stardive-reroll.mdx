If you are a hardcore player aiming for the limited character **Esther**, the **mongil stardive reroll** process becomes significantly more difficult. Esther is not on the beginner banner and has a low 1% drop rate on her limited recruitment banner.

To attempt an Esther reroll, you must progress past the initial hub to the "Scrubs Up, Everyone" quest. This unlocks the Event System and provides a small amount of **Compass of Destiny** currency. You can also exchange **Threads of Promise** (obtained from duplicate pulls) in the shop for extra event summons. However, this process takes approximately 20-30 minutes and has a very low success rate. Most players are advised to stick to the standard reroll and save their crystals to hit the 76-pull soft pity on Esther's banner naturally.

## FAQ

**Q: How long does a single mongil stardive reroll take?**

A: A standard reroll takes about 10 to 15 minutes if you skip all cutscenes and play on the "Path of Dawn" difficulty setting.

**Q: Can I use the same email address for multiple rerolls?**

A: No. Because of the 30-day deletion cooldown, you must use a different email address or social media account for every attempt.

**Q: Is it worth rerolling for limited characters like Esther?**

A: It is generally not recommended unless you have a lot of patience. The drop rate is only 1%, and the currency for limited banners is scarce in the early game. It is much more efficient to reroll for a top-tier standard unit like Jiwon.

**Q: What should I do with the "Threads of Promise" I get from pulling?**

A: These can be exchanged in the shop for "Compass of Destiny" (limited pulls) or high-tier Artifacts. For rerollers, exchanging them for extra pulls to try and snag a limited character is a common strategy.
