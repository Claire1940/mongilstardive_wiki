## FAQ

**Q: Does the mongil star dive daily reset affect the pity counter on banners?**

A: No, the daily reset does not reset your pity counter. Pity is persistent across the specific banner's duration. However, the daily reset provides the missions necessary to earn the currency required to reach that pity.

**Q: What happens if I am logged in during the daily reset?**

A: In most cases, you will receive a notification that the "Day has changed." You may need to return to the main title screen or reload the Gathering Hub to see the new daily missions and updated shop inventories.

**Q: Are daily rewards lost if I don't claim them before the reset?**

A: Yes. Any unclaimed rewards from completed daily missions are typically lost once the **mongil star dive daily reset** occurs. Always make it a habit to click "Claim All" before logging off for the night.

**Q: Does the server reset time change for Daylight Savings?**

A: The server time itself usually remains fixed (e.g., UTC+0), but your local time relative to that reset will shift by one hour if your region observes Daylight Savings. Always double-check your local clock against the server's UTC offset.
