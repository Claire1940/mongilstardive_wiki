## Risks and Online Safety Protocols

As with any game featuring online connectivity and a "Monster Tamers" guild system, using **mongil stardive mods** comes with inherent risks. Netmarble maintains strict policies regarding game integrity, especially concerning modifications that could provide a competitive advantage.

1.  **Avoid Script Mods:** Any mod that alters the "magic stone" drop rates or monster stats is likely to result in a permanent ban.
2.  **Stick to Client-Side Visuals:** Aesthetic mods that only change what you see on your screen are generally safer, though never officially supported.
3.  **Check for Updates:** Official game patches often break mod functionality. Always check the official [Mongil: Star Dive website](https://stardive.netmarble.com/en/) for patch notes before re-enabling your mods.
4.  **Use Secondary Accounts:** If you are testing a new, unverified mod, consider using a secondary account to prevent your main progress from being compromised.

## FAQ

**Q: Are mongil stardive mods legal to use?**

A: Using mods is generally a violation of the Terms of Service for most online games. While visual mods are often ignored by developers, they are never "legal" or officially sanctioned. Use them at your own risk.

**Q: Where can I find the best mongil stardive mods?**

A: Most mods are hosted on community-driven platforms like Nexus Mods or dedicated Discord servers for the game. Always ensure you are downloading from a reputable source with positive user feedback.

**Q: Will mods work on the mobile version of the game?**

A: Modding the mobile version is significantly more difficult than the PC version. Most **mongil stardive mods** are designed specifically for the Windows client due to the ease of file access.

**Q: Can mods help me catch legendary monsters faster?**

A: Any mod that claims to change catch rates or drop rates is a "cheat" and will likely get your account banned. Stick to visual and performance modifications to stay safe.
